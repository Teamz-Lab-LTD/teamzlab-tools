What Is a Click Speed Test?

A click speed test measures how many times you can click your mouse button within a set time period, expressed as clicks per second (CPS). This metric is important for competitive gaming, especially in titles like Minecraft PvP, osu!, and various FPS games where rapid clicking can give you an advantage.

How CPS Is Calculated

CPS Formula

CPS = Total Clicks / Duration (seconds)

Example: 45 clicks in 5 seconds = 9.0 CPS

Click Speed Ratings

Click speeds vary widely among gamers. Here is how different CPS ranges are typically categorized:

  • Casual (below 4 CPS): Normal everyday clicking speed
  • Average (4-7 CPS): Typical for regular gamers
  • Fast (7-10 CPS): Above average, good for most competitive games
  • Very Fast (10-13 CPS): Jitter clicking range, excellent for PvP
  • Inhuman (13+ CPS): Butterfly clicking or drag clicking territory

Clicking Techniques

Advanced gamers use specialized clicking techniques to achieve higher CPS. Jitter clicking involves tensing your arm muscles to vibrate your finger rapidly on the mouse button, typically achieving 10-14 CPS. Butterfly clicking alternates two fingers on the same button to reach 15-25 CPS. Drag clicking involves dragging your finger across the button to register multiple clicks from friction.

Tips to Improve Click Speed

  • Practice regularly with short timed sessions
  • Use a mouse with a light click actuation force
  • Keep your hand relaxed to avoid fatigue
  • Try different grip styles (palm, claw, fingertip)
  • Warm up your hands before testing
