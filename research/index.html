Research Paper & Publishing Tools

Everything you need to write, format, and publish academic papers — citation generators, bibliography makers, DOI lookup, outline builders, abstract writers, and methodology selectors. All tools run in your browser with zero data collection.

Citation Generator

Generate perfectly formatted citations in APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE, and Vancouver styles. Supports books, journals, websites, and conference papers.

Bibliography Generator

Create complete bibliographies from multiple sources. Add sources one by one, choose your style, and export as formatted text or BibTeX.

Research Paper Outline Generator

Generate structured outlines for any paper type — argumentative, analytical, experimental, or literature review. Tailored by discipline.

DOI Lookup Tool

Look up any research paper by DOI number. Get title, authors, journal, year, abstract, and auto-generated citations from CrossRef.

Research Paper Title Generator

Generate compelling, discipline-specific paper titles from your topic and keywords. Get 10 variations in different academic styles.

Abstract Generator

Create structured research paper abstracts with background, methods, results, and conclusion sections. Respects word limits.

Thesis Statement Generator

Build strong thesis statements for argumentative, analytical, and expository papers. Get 5 variations with strength scoring.

Literature Review Matrix

Organize your literature review sources in a structured matrix. Track authors, methods, findings, and themes. Export as CSV.

Research Methodology Selector

Answer guided questions to find the best research methodology for your study. Get matched recommendations with strengths and limitations.

Cover Page Generator

Create properly formatted cover pages for APA, MLA, Chicago, and IEEE papers. Live preview and download as PNG or PDF.
