How the Senior Deduction Works

Starting with the 2025 tax year, taxpayers aged 65 and older can claim an additional deduction that helps reduce the taxable portion of their Social Security benefits. This is separate from and in addition to the standard deduction and the existing age-based standard deduction increase.

Senior Deduction Limits and Phase-Outs

Quick Reference (2025–2028 Tax Years)

  • Maximum deduction (single/HOH): $6,000
  • Maximum deduction (married filing jointly): $12,000
  • Phase-out starts (single/HOH): $75,000 MAGI
  • Phase-out starts (MFJ): $150,000 MAGI
  • Fully phases out (single/HOH): $175,000 MAGI
  • Fully phases out (MFJ): $250,000 MAGI
  • Available for: tax years 2025 through 2028
  • Must be age 65 or older by end of tax year
