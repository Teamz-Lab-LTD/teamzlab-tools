Writing a research paper is hard enough without fighting with citation formats, outline structures, and cover page layouts.

I built **10 free research paper & publishing tools** that run entirely in your browser — no signup, no uploads, no data collection. Your research stays private.

## What's included?

**Citation & Bibliography:**
- [Citation Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/citation-generator/) — APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago 17, Harvard, IEEE, Vancouver. Enter source details, get all 6 formats at once
- [Bibliography Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/bibliography-generator/) — Add multiple sources, generate a complete bibliography, export as BibTeX
- [DOI Lookup Tool](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/doi-lookup/) — Paste a DOI, get the full paper metadata + auto-generated citations from CrossRef

**Writing & Structure:**
- [Research Paper Outline Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/research-paper-outline-generator/) — Pick your paper type (experimental, argumentative, literature review) and discipline. Get a detailed hierarchical outline
- [Abstract Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/abstract-generator/) — Enter your findings and methodology, get a structured abstract with background, methods, results, conclusion
- [Thesis Statement Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/thesis-statement-generator/) — 5 thesis variations per topic with strength scoring and improvement tips
- [Research Paper Title Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/title-generator/) — 10 discipline-specific title suggestions per topic

**Research Planning:**
- [Literature Review Matrix](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/literature-review-matrix/) — Organize sources in a sortable table with relevance scoring. Export as CSV
- [Research Methodology Selector](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/methodology-selector/) — Take a 7-question quiz, get matched with the best methodology for your study
- [Cover Page Generator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/research/cover-page-generator/) — APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE formatted cover pages with live preview. Download as PNG or PDF

## AI-enhanced (where it helps)

Four of the tools (Abstract, Title, Thesis, Outline) use a 3-tier AI system:
1. Chrome AI (if available in your browser)
2. Transformers.js (runs a model locally — works offline after first download)
3. Rule-based templates (always works, no AI needed)

The AI enhances output quality when available, but the tools work perfectly fine without it.
