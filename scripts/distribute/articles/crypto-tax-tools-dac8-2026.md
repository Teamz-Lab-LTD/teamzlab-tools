DAC8 went live on January 1, 2026. If you hold crypto in the EU, your exchange is now automatically reporting your trades to tax authorities. Italy raised its rate to 33%. CARF is rolling out across 40+ countries.

I built 4 free tools to help crypto investors navigate this new landscape. No signup, no data collection — everything runs in your browser.

## 1. Crypto Tax Calculator by Country (40+ Countries)

The #1 question every crypto investor asks: "How much tax do I owe?"

This tool covers **42 countries** with 2026 rates:
- Select your country, enter your gains, pick short-term or long-term
- See your estimated tax, effective rate, and net after tax
- DAC8 and CARF badges show which reporting frameworks apply to you
- Tax-free countries highlighted (UAE, Singapore, Switzerland, Hong Kong)

Key data points that surprised me building this:
- **Japan** taxes crypto at up to **55%** (highest in the world)
- **Germany** is **0%** if you hold over 1 year
- **Italy** jumped from 26% to **33%** in January 2026
- **Indonesia** charges only **0.1%** per transaction

## 3. Fear & Greed DCA Backtester

"Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful" — but does it actually work for DCA?

This tool backtests two strategies against real historical data (2018-2025):
- **Regular DCA**: Buy every month regardless of market sentiment
- **Fear DCA**: Only buy when the Fear & Greed Index is below your threshold

Set your monthly amount, threshold (default 25 = Extreme Fear), and date range. See side-by-side comparison of total invested, BTC accumulated, ROI, and average buy price.

Spoiler: the results depend heavily on the time period. Fear DCA crushes regular DCA in periods that include crashes (2018, 2022). Regular DCA wins during sustained bull runs.

**Try it:** [Fear & Greed DCA Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/crypto/fear-greed-dca-calculator/)
