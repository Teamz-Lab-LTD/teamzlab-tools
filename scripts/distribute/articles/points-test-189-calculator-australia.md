# Free Points Test 189 Calculator Australia

If you are planning an Australian `Subclass 189` application, the first question is usually not “which visa should I pick?”

It is:

- Do I clear the `65-point` threshold?
- Am I actually competitive for `189` without nomination points?
- Do I still have blockers such as English level, occupation-list fit, or skills assessment?

That is why I shipped a focused browser-based tool instead of another generic migration article.

## What this calculator does

This tool is built specifically for the `Skilled Independent Subclass 189` route.

It helps you:

- estimate your current points total
- check whether you pass the `65-point` EOI threshold
- see a points breakdown across age, English, work experience, qualifications, and bonus categories
- flag common blockers before you treat your profile as `189-ready`

Unlike a broad PR page, this one is intentionally narrow.

It does **not** add `190` or `491` nomination points, because the point is to answer the more specific question:

“What does my score look like if I am targeting `189` only?”

## Why this is useful

Many people reach the minimum threshold on paper but still are not ready to lodge a serious `189` Expression of Interest.

Typical reasons:

- they have not confirmed the skilled occupation list
- they do not yet have a suitable skills assessment
- their English score is below the category they thought they had
- they are mixing `189` assumptions with `190` or `491` nomination points

This calculator surfaces those issues early.
