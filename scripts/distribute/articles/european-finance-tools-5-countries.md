## Why Localized?

Most finance calculators online are in English only. By building tools in German, Danish, and Finnish, we serve users who prefer calculating in their native language — with country-specific tax rates, deductions, and regulations that generic tools don't cover.

All 40 tools are part of [Teamz Lab Tools](https://tool.teamzlab.com/) — 1,400+ free browser tools. No signup, no data collection, everything runs locally.
