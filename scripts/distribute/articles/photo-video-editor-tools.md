Apply Instagram-style filters to any photo. 37 filters across 7 categories (vintage, warm, cool, noir, artistic, color pop, B&W). Real-time preview with intensity slider.

### [Photo Collage Maker](https://tool.teamzlab.com/image/photo-collage-maker/)
Create photo collages with 12+ layout templates. Upload 2-9 photos, customize gaps, borders, and background. Export in Square, Portrait, Landscape, or A4.

12 adjustment sliders: brightness, contrast, saturation, hue, temperature, tint, highlights, shadows, sharpness, vignette, fade, grain. AI auto-enhance with histogram analysis.

## Video Editing Tools

### [Screen Recorder](https://tool.teamzlab.com/video/screen-recorder/)
Record screen, webcam, or both. 4 modes with audio options, quality settings (720p-4K), countdown timer, and PiP webcam overlay. Download as WebM.

### [Video Compressor](https://tool.teamzlab.com/video/video-compressor/)
Compress videos to target sizes (10MB for email, 25MB for Discord, custom). Powered by FFmpeg.wasm — all processing in your browser. Before/after size comparison.

### [Video to GIF Converter](https://tool.teamzlab.com/video/video-to-gif/)
Convert any video clip to animated GIF. Select start/end time, adjust frame rate and size. Uses 2-pass palette generation for high-quality output.

### [Video Speed Changer](https://tool.teamzlab.com/video/video-speed-changer/)
Speed up (2x, 3x, 4x) or slow down (0.25x, 0.5x) any video. Real-time preview with HTML5 video, export via FFmpeg.wasm.

### [Video Trimmer](https://tool.teamzlab.com/video/video-trimmer/)
Trim videos with a visual timeline and draggable handles. Fast mode (instant, copy) or precise mode (frame-accurate, re-encode).
