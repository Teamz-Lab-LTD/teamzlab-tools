# I Built 1,829 Free Browser Tools — Here's the Complete Directory

Most "free" tools online are anything but free. They want your email. They track you. They let you try 3 times before hitting a paywall.

I got tired of it.

So I built [Teamz Lab Tools](https://tool.teamzlab.com) — a collection of **1,829 browser-based calculators, generators, and utilities** across 98 categories and 30+ countries. Every single one runs entirely in your browser. No backend. No sign-up. No cookies. No data ever leaves your device.

This is the complete directory. Bookmark it.

---

## How It Works

Every tool is pure client-side JavaScript. When you open a calculator, your browser does the math — not a server. That means:

- **Zero data collection** — your inputs stay on your device
- **Works offline** — once loaded, most tools run without internet
- **No accounts** — just open and use, instantly
- **No rate limits** — calculate 1,000 times, nobody cares
- **Mobile-friendly** — responsive design, works on any screen

Now, here's everything organized by category.

## The Tech Behind It

For anyone curious about the technical architecture:

- **Static site** hosted on GitHub Pages — zero server costs
- **No framework** — vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript for maximum speed
- **Service worker** for offline support
- **CSS custom properties** (design tokens) for consistent theming across 1,829 pages
- **Dark/light mode** that persists across sessions
- **Auto-save** — every input is saved to localStorage, so returning users see their last values
- **Share links** — generate a URL that reconstructs your exact inputs (no server needed)
- **3 JSON-LD schemas** on every page (BreadcrumbList, FAQPage, WebApplication) for rich Google results

The entire site is open to crawlers. No login walls. No JavaScript-rendered content that blocks Googlebot.
