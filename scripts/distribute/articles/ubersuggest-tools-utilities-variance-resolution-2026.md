## 1. Utilities Cost Calculator — Know Where Your Money Goes

The average American household spends $449/month on utilities. Do you know your breakdown?

The [Utilities Cost Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/housing/utilities-cost-calculator/) lets you enter costs for electricity, gas, water, internet, phone, trash, and streaming. You get:

- Monthly total with pie chart breakdown
- Annual projection and daily cost
- Comparison against national averages (are you overpaying?)
- Percentage of income analysis
- Category-specific tips to reduce each bill

If your utilities eat more than 10% of your income, the tool flags it — along with actionable tips to cut costs.

## 2. Percent Variance Calculator — Budget vs Actual in Seconds

Every business does budget variance analysis. Most people do it in Excel. Why?

The [Percent Variance Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/math/percent-variance-calculator/) handles it instantly:

- Enter expected vs actual values
- Get variance %, absolute difference, and direction
- Batch mode: paste multiple pairs, calculate all at once
- Three contexts: Budget vs Actual, Price Change, Performance
- Shows the formula with YOUR numbers — great for reports

Copy the result and paste it straight into your report or spreadsheet.

## 3. Resolution Checker — Screen + Image Resolution Detection

Need to know your screen resolution? Want to check an image's dimensions?

The [Resolution Checker](https://tool.teamzlab.com/diagnostic/resolution-checker/) auto-detects everything on load:

- Screen resolution and viewport size
- Device pixel ratio (Retina detection)
- Aspect ratio and color depth
- Which standard your screen matches (HD, Full HD, QHD, 4K, 5K)
- **Upload an image** to check its dimensions, megapixels, and file size
- Live resize detection — shrink your browser and watch values update

No permissions needed. Works on any device.
