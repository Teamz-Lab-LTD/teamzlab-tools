Most "free AI tools" send your data to a server, require signups, or hit you with usage limits after 3 tries.

I built a collection of AI-powered tools that run **entirely in your browser** — your data never leaves your device. No API keys, no accounts, no limits.

## How it works

These tools use two approaches:
- **Chrome's built-in AI** (Chrome 138+) — models run natively in the browser
- **Transformers.js** — open-source AI models that download once and work offline forever

Either way, your text, images, and data stay on your machine.

## Why privacy matters for AI tools

When you paste your resume into an AI rewriter, that text goes to a server. When you upload a photo for background removal, that image gets stored somewhere. When you check your writing for tone, someone else is reading it.

These tools eliminate that concern. The AI model runs in your browser tab. Close the tab and everything is gone.
