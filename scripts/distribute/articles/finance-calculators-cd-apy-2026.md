- See total interest earned vs principal in a visual donut chart
- **Compare up to 3 CDs side-by-side** -- most calculators don't do this

The comparison mode is the killer feature. Drop in your top 3 CD offers and instantly see which one wins.

## 2. APY Calculator -- Because APR and APY Are Not the Same Thing

A savings account advertising 4.50% APR with daily compounding actually earns you **4.60% APY**. That's free money most people miss.

The [APY Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/evergreen/apy-calculator/) has three modes:
- **Calculate APY** from any interest rate and compounding frequency
- **APR to APY converter** -- see how compounding frequency changes your earnings
- **Compare rates** -- put 3 accounts side-by-side with 1-year and 5-year projections

## 3. CD vs Savings Account Comparison -- The Question Everyone Asks

"Should I lock my money in a CD or keep it in a high-yield savings account?"

The [CD vs Savings Comparison](https://tool.teamzlab.com/evergreen/cd-vs-savings-comparison/) tool answers this with math, not opinions:
- Side-by-side comparison with identical deposit amounts
- Growth chart showing both accounts over time
- **"What if rates change?" toggle** -- savings rates are variable, CDs are fixed. See what happens if your savings APY drops mid-term
- Plain English verdict: which option wins and by how much
