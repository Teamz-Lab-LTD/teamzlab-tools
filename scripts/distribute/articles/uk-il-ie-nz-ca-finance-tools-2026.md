## New Zealand — Foreign Investment Fund (FIF) Tax Calculator

NZ taxes foreign shares under the FIF regime — either the Fair Dividend Rate (5% of opening value) or Comparative Value method. Many NZ investors overpay because they don't know which method to use.

This calculator computes your FIF tax under both methods and tells you which one is lower, so you can elect the right one at tax time.

**Try it:** [NZ FIF Tax Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/nz/foreign-investment-fund-calculator/)

## Israel — Income Tax Calculator
