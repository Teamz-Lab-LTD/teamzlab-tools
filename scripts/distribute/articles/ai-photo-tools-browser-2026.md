## AI Image Upscaler — 2x and 4x Without the Blur

Most free upscalers just interpolate pixels, giving you a larger but blurrier image. The [AI Image Upscaler](https://tool.teamzlab.com/image/ai-image-upscaler/) uses Real-ESRGAN via Transformers.js to reconstruct detail rather than guess at it.

- 2x and 4x upscaling modes
- Works on photos, illustrations, and screenshots
- Runs via WebAssembly — model downloads once, works offline after that
- No size limits, no watermarks
- Download at full resolution

Free alternative to Topaz Gigapixel AI and Let's Enhance.

- Detects and repairs scratches, tears, and dust
- Recovers faded tones and improves sharpness
- Works on grayscale and sepia photographs
- Before/after comparison slider
- Download restored image at original resolution

Comparable to MyHeritage Photo Enhancer and Remini for old photos — except free and private.

## AI Image Noise Remover — Clean Up Grainy Photos

Low-light photos and high-ISO shots pick up digital noise: that grainy, speckled look that ruins otherwise good images. The [AI Image Noise Remover](https://tool.teamzlab.com/image/ai-image-noise-remover/) applies learned denoise filters to smooth grain while preserving edge detail.

- Removes luminance and color noise
- Strength slider (light to aggressive)
- Preserves fine detail in hair, fabric, and foliage
- Side-by-side comparison
- Works on JPG, PNG, and WebP

Free alternative to Topaz DeNoise AI and DxO PureRAW.

- Detects and enhances faces automatically
- Restores eyes, skin texture, and hair detail
- Works on old photos, screenshots, and video stills
- Adjustable enhancement strength
- Download full-resolution result

Best for: restoring old portraits, fixing blurry group photos, enhancing profile pictures.
