## [Capital Gains Tax Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/us/capital-gains-tax-calculator/)

Calculate federal capital gains tax on stocks, crypto, and real estate investments.

**Features:**
- Short-term vs long-term capital gains rates (2026 brackets)
- All filing statuses: Single, MFJ, MFS, Head of Household
- Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT) 3.8% surtax calculation
- Multiple asset support — add all your investments at once
- Visual pie chart showing profit vs tax breakdown
- Handles capital losses with $3,000/year deduction note

**Tax rates covered:**
- Short-term: 10%, 12%, 22%, 24%, 32%, 35%, 37% (ordinary income)
- Long-term: 0%, 15%, or 20% based on income bracket
- NIIT: Additional 3.8% for high earners ($200K+ single / $250K+ MFJ)

## [401k Growth Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/us/401k-growth-calculator/)

Project your 401k balance at retirement with employer match, compound growth, and salary increases.

**Features:**
- 11 inputs covering every variable that affects 401k growth
- Year-by-year projection with IRS contribution limits
- Catch-up contributions automatically applied at age 50+
- Interactive line chart showing balance growth over time
- Pie chart breaking down: your contributions vs employer match vs investment growth
- "What if +1%?" scenario — see how much extra 1% contribution adds over your career
- Full year-by-year breakdown table

**Example:** A 30-year-old earning $75K contributing 6% with a 50% employer match at 7% return = approximately $1.2M by age 65. Increase to 7%? That one percent adds ~$100K+ to your retirement.
