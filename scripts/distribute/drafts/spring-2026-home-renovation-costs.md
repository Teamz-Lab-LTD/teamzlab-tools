## The Renovation Math Most People Skip

Before you spend $30K on a kitchen, run these numbers:

1. **What's your home worth now?** → Use your latest assessment
2. **What will it be worth after?** → Kitchen adds ~75% of cost. $30K kitchen = ~$22K added value
3. **Are you staying or selling?** → If staying 10+ years, renovate for yourself. If selling within 3 years, focus on ROI
4. **Can you actually afford it?** → The [Mortgage Calculator](https://tool.teamzlab.com/evergreen/mortgage-calculator/) shows what a HELOC payment looks like on your budget

**30% of homeowners went into debt for renovations they couldn't afford.** Don't be that statistic. Run the calculators first.
