The Problem We Solve

Care homes must prove to government inspectors that they provide safe, quality care. But their evidence is scattered across WhatsApp messages, paper notes, Excel files, and emails. When an inspector arrives, managers spend hours pulling records together. Many facilities fail inspections — not because of bad care, but because of bad documentation.

This is a universal problem across 4 countries:

  • UK: The Care Quality Commission (CQC) inspects 29,700+ care homes
  • Australia: The Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission assesses 3,600+ facilities under new ACQS 2025 standards
  • New Zealand: Designated Auditing Agencies certify 900+ rest homes under NZS 8134
  • Ireland: HIQA inspects 580+ nursing homes under National Standards

What AlwaysReady Care Does

One simple app that records, organises, and exports compliance evidence. It works alongside whatever system the care home already uses — no need to replace anything. Think of it as a "compliance layer" on top of their existing setup.

1

Record Evidence (60 seconds)

A care worker opens the app on their phone. They pick a template (medication, personal care, meals, incidents, etc.), fill in what happened, and submit. Done in under a minute.

2

AI Auto-Tags It

The app reads the note and automatically figures out which compliance category it belongs to (e.g., "Medication Management" or "Falls Prevention"). It also flags any risks — like a fall or an injury — and suggests follow-up actions.

3

Manager Reviews

Senior staff review the evidence. Good records get approved. Incomplete ones get sent back with feedback. Only approved evidence counts towards the compliance score.

4

Dashboard Shows Readiness

A live dashboard shows a percentage score — "You are 67% ready. These 7 areas have gaps." The manager knows exactly what to fix before an inspector arrives.

5

One-Click Inspection Pack

When the inspector arrives, the manager clicks one button and downloads a professional evidence pack — all approved records, organised and ready to hand over.
