Caring

Dignity, privacy, respect, and involvement.

What this Caring covers

Caring asks whether staff treat people with compassion and respect. It is observational — inspectors watch interactions and interview residents.

Evidence requirements

AreaWhat inspectors look for
DignityPrivacy respected; personal care private; resident-directed.
InvolvementResidents involved in care decisions.
Emotional supportLife stories recorded; bereavement support available.
CommunicationInformation accessible; reasonable adjustments made.

Common pitfalls

  • Institutional language / routines
  • Staff discussing residents within earshot
  • Care done TO rather than WITH residents.
