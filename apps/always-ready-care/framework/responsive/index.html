Responsive

Person-centred care and complaints.

What this Responsive covers

Responsive asks if the service responds to individual needs, preferences and complaints. It includes activities, end-of-life care and reasonable adjustments.

Evidence requirements

AreaWhat inspectors look for
Person-centred careCare reflects individual preferences, not routines.
ActivitiesWeekly programme with attendance logs.
ComplaintsLog with actions and timescales; learning evidenced.
End-of-lifeAdvance care planning; palliative support.

Common pitfalls

  • One-size-fits-all activity programmes
  • Complaints resolved without learning
  • No advance care plans.
