FAQ — 8 National Standards themes

What are the 8 HIQA National Standards themes?

Person-Centred Care, Effective Services, Safe Services, Health & Wellbeing, Leadership/Governance/Management, Workforce, Use of Resources, Use of Information.

Are they specific to nursing homes?

They apply to designated centres for older people. Different standards sets apply to disability centres.

What is the HIQA Judgment Framework?

Inspector judgments: Compliant / Substantially Compliant / Not Compliant / Not Compliant major — used to rate each relevant regulation during inspection.

Does this cover the Care of Older People regulations?

Yes — Theme-level, not regulation-level. For reg-level detail see hiqa.ie.
