FAQ — Ngā Paerewa standards

What is Ngā Paerewa?

The Māori name for NZS 8134:2021 — integrating te Tiriti o Waitangi principles throughout the Health and Disability Services Standards.

Who audits these standards?

Designated Auditing Agencies (DAAs) approved by the Ministry of Health / Manatū Hauora, not the Ministry directly.

How often are certification audits?

Every 3 years by default; risk-based sooner if issues are identified.

Are the standards compulsory?

Yes — certification against NZS 8134 is a condition of operating health and disability services including rest homes and aged residential care.
