SASSA Grant Types and Amounts

The South African Social Security Agency (SASSA) administers several grant types, each targeting a specific demographic. The Social Relief of Distress (SRD) grant provides R370 per month to unemployed South Africans aged 18 to 59 with no income. The Old Age Pension pays R2,190 per month to citizens over 60 years of age. The Disability Grant, also R2,190, supports individuals with confirmed disabilities that prevent them from working. The Child Support Grant provides R530 per month per child for primary caregivers, and the Foster Care Grant pays R1,180 per month for registered foster parents.

SASSA typically pays grants on different dates within the first week of each month. Old Age grants are usually paid first (around the 3rd), followed by Disability grants (around the 4th), and then other grants including Child Support and SRD. The exact dates vary each month and are announced by SASSA in advance. Planning your budget around these dates ensures you can allocate funds properly for the entire month.

Budget Planning Formulas

Daily Budget: Grant Amount ÷ Days in Month

Days Until Payment: Next Payment Date − Today

Expense Coverage: Grant Amount ≥ Monthly Expenses?

Tips for Managing Your Grant Between Payment Dates

The period between grant payments can be challenging. Divide your grant amount by the number of days in the month to determine your daily budget. Prioritise spending on food and essential medications. If possible, buy staple food items in bulk right after receiving your grant to save money. Many SASSA beneficiaries collect their grants at retail stores where they can immediately purchase groceries, avoiding the temptation of spending the cash on non-essentials. Consider keeping a simple written budget log to track daily spending and ensure money lasts the full month.

Checking Your SASSA Payment Status

You can check your grant status and payment dates through the SASSA website, the SASSA Toll-Free number 0800 601 011, or by visiting your nearest SASSA office. For the SRD grant, use the SASSA SRD status check page at srd.sassa.gov.za. Keep your ID number and phone number ready when checking. If your grant is delayed, contact SASSA immediately to resolve any issues with your application or payment method.
