About Teamz Lab Tools

Teamz Lab Tools is a collection of free, instant-use online calculators designed to help professionals, freelancers, and employees with everyday work-related calculations.

Our Mission

We believe practical tools should be free, fast, and frictionless. No sign-ups. No downloads. No data collection. Just enter your numbers and get instant, accurate results.

What We Offer

Our tools cover two core areas:

  • Work & Payroll — Notice period calculators, leave encashment, salary breakdowns, probation end dates, and more.
  • Invoice & Freelancing — Invoice due dates, late fees, freelance rate estimation, project pricing, and retainer calculators.

Every calculator runs entirely in your browser. Your data stays on your device — nothing is sent to any server.
