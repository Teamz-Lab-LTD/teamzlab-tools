## Research Notes

### Financial Planning Tools (2026 trends)
- **NZ mortgage market is hot**: Record $5.8B in switching lending (Dec 2025), 41% of all NZ lending is now bank-switching. NZ floating rate 5.66%, 1yr fixed 4.70% — "fix or float" is the #1 question for NZ homeowners right now.
- **Singapore property**: HDB affordability calculators with TDSR/MSR rules + CPF grant integration are heavily searched. 99.co and PropertyGuru dominate; there's a gap for a simpler tool targeted at first-timers.
- **Australia CGT**: Searches for CGT calculators spike each May-June (EOFY). The 50% CGT discount for assets held 12+ months is a key differentiator AU searchers need explained alongside the calculator.
- **Ireland Rent Tax Credit**: Introduced 2022, expanded 2024 — many renters still don't know they qualify. Searches for "rent tax credit calculator ireland" are rising.
- **UK self-employed (2026/27)**: Class 2 NI abolished from April 2024. New Class 4 rates (6%/2%) mean old calculators are wrong. Tax thresholds frozen until April 2028 — plenty of confused freelancers searching.

### AI Career Tools (2026 trends)
- 87% of companies now use AI in some part of recruitment; 93% plan to expand usage.
- Applicants using AI in job search 60% more likely to land higher-paying roles.
- **Salary negotiation** is the #1 underserved career tool gap — users want a script generator and simulation, not just a data point.
- **Behavioural interview answer generator** (STAR method) is heavily searched; existing tools are mostly SaaS/paid.
- ATS-aware **LinkedIn headline generators** are rising — LinkedIn profile optimization is a distinct market from resume optimization.
- Equity/RSU job offer comparisons are highly searched in US/UK tech markets.

### Legal Tools (2026 trends)
- AI contract review tools achieving 25-50% faster processing; 94% accuracy on NDAs.
- **Freelance contract generator** remains underserved for client-side (freelancer generates the contract). Existing tools are mostly lawyer-directed.
- **NDA enforceability is tightening** — 2026 contract updates now need GDPR/data-processing clauses. Updated NDA generators targeting this will rank well.
- Remote work policy templates are a new B2B need post-2025: employers scrambling to codify hybrid/WFH rules.

### Productivity / Health (2026 trends)
- 86% of full-time remote workers report burnout — the burnout market is real and growing.
- Research shows remote workers get only 2-3 hours of deep focus daily — a "deep work optimizer" addresses this pain point.
- TDEE/macro calculators are saturated. **Do not build** — already covered by /health/tdee-calculator/ and /health/macro-calculator/.
- Niche health tools with B2B angle (remote worker burnout, deep work hours) have higher RPM because they attract productivity/HR audience.

### Small Business HR (2026 trends)
- **True cost of hiring** is a perennial high-RPM search — employers want to know total employer cost beyond salary (NI, pension, benefits, onboarding).
- US payroll tax estimator for small businesses (sub-50 employees) is highly searched; existing tools are behind SaaS paywalls.
- AU/UK versions of employee cost calculators are needed because employer NI/super rates differ significantly from the US.
