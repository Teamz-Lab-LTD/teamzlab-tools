# India-Specific Tool Strategy

**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Target:** India (1.4B population, English-language tools with Indian financial terms)

## Why India Works
- Massive daily search volume for financial calculators
- Most existing tools have poor UX (slow, ad-heavy, cluttered)
- Recurring need: tax season, salary negotiations, investment planning, loan EMIs
- Lower ad RPM but compensated by massive volume
- Indian searches are mostly in English

## Top India Tools (by search volume)
1. CTC to In-Hand Salary Calculator (extremely high search)
2. Income Tax Calculator - Old vs New Regime (seasonal peak)
3. HRA Exemption Calculator (every salaried employee)
4. EPF/PF Calculator (every month)
5. SIP Calculator (investing trend)
6. EMI Calculator (loans)
7. FD Calculator (fixed deposits)
8. PPF Calculator (savings)
9. GST Calculator (every business)
10. TDS Calculator (every quarter)
11. NPS Tax Benefit Calculator
12. Section 80C Tax Savings Calculator
13. Home Loan Tax Benefit Calculator
14. Professional Tax Calculator (state-wise)
15. Sukanya Samriddhi Calculator
