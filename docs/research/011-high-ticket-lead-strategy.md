# High-Ticket Lead Generation Strategy — Deep Research #11

**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Source:** ChatGPT Deep Research (Lead-gen for Teamz Lab)

## Target Verticals (ranked)
1. Field service companies (HVAC, electrical, plumbing, cleaning, lift maintenance)
2. Property management / facilities companies
3. B2B SaaS companies needing AI + product design help
4. Logistics / warehouse / 3PL operators
5. Private healthcare groups / clinic networks

## What to Sell per Vertical
- Field service: job scheduling dashboard, engineer mobile app, quote-to-invoice workflow, AI dispatch assistant
- Property: tenant portal, maintenance ticket system, inspection app, AI maintenance triage
- B2B SaaS: UX redesign, AI feature integration, admin dashboard rebuild, design system execution
- Logistics: shipment exception dashboard, warehouse KPI portal, AI customer-service assistant
- Healthcare: patient intake portal, ops dashboard, AI note summarization

## Who to Contact
- Operations Director, COO, Service Director, Head of Dispatch, Practice Manager, VP Product, CTO

## Positioning
"We help service businesses and product teams replace messy manual workflows with clean internal dashboards, mobile apps, client portals, and AI-assisted operations tools."

## Lead Magnet Tool Clusters to Build
- Service Pricing / Quote / ROI Calculator Hub
- Field Service Efficiency Calculator Hub
- Property Operations ROI Calculator Hub
- AI Feature Cost / ROI Calculator Hub

## Avoid
- Tiny local restaurants, small generic e-commerce, unfunded idea-stage founders, enterprise work at SME budgets
