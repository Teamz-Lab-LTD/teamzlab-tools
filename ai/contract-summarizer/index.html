How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Summarizer API, which runs Gemini Nano directly on your device. Your contract text is never sent to any server — it stays on your computer the entire time. This makes it safe for sensitive legal documents, NDAs, employment contracts, and business agreements.

If Chrome AI is not available (different browser, older version, or insufficient hardware), the tool falls back to a rule-based extraction that identifies dates, dollar amounts, named parties, and key legal terms.

What Gets Extracted

  • Key points — Main obligations, rights, and conditions
  • Dates & deadlines — Effective dates, termination, renewal
  • Financial terms — Amounts, payment schedules, penalties
  • Parties — Who is involved and their roles
  • Important clauses — Non-compete, confidentiality, liability

Privacy-First AI

Unlike cloud-based AI tools that upload your text to remote servers, this tool processes everything locally. Your sensitive contract data never leaves your browser. This is possible because Chrome downloads and runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware.
