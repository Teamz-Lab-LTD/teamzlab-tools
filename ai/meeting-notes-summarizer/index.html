How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Summarizer API with the key-points type to distill your meeting notes into actionable summaries. The AI identifies the most important discussion points, decisions made, and action items assigned.

If Chrome AI is not available, the tool uses keyword-based extraction to find lines mentioning actions, todos, deadlines, decisions, owners, and follow-ups.

What Gets Extracted

  • Key decisions — What was agreed or decided during the meeting
  • Action items — Tasks assigned with owners and deadlines
  • Discussion points — Main topics covered
  • Follow-ups — Items needing future attention

Privacy-First AI

Meeting notes often contain sensitive business information. This tool processes everything locally on your device — your notes never leave your browser. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model on your hardware for complete privacy.
