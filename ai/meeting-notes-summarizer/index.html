How the Meeting Notes Summarizer Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Summarizer API with the key-points type to distill your meeting notes into actionable summaries. The AI identifies discussion points, decisions made, and action items assigned. When Chrome AI is unavailable, the tool uses keyword-based extraction to find lines mentioning actions, todos, deadlines, decisions, and follow-ups. Paste any format of notes including bullet points, paragraphs, transcripts, or structured minutes and get organized output in seconds.

What Gets Extracted from Your Notes

The summarizer focuses on four categories that matter most after a meeting. Key decisions capture what was agreed or approved during discussion. Action items list tasks with owners and deadlines so nothing falls through the cracks. Discussion points outline the main topics covered for anyone who missed the meeting. Follow-ups flag items needing future attention or revisiting. This structured output replaces hours of manual note cleanup and ensures every team member knows exactly what to do next.

Private and Secure Meeting Summaries

Meeting notes often contain sensitive business information including financial data, strategic plans, and personnel decisions. This tool processes everything locally on your device, and your notes never leave your browser. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model on your hardware for complete privacy. Unlike cloud-based summarizers that upload your text to remote servers, this approach ensures confidential meeting content stays confidential. The keyword-based fallback also runs entirely in your browser's JavaScript engine.
