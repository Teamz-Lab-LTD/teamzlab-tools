How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Rewriter API to analyze your email draft and produce an improved version. The AI enhances clarity, fixes awkward phrasing, improves tone, and tightens structure — all while preserving your original message.

If Chrome AI is not available, the tool provides a fallback analysis with word count, sentence statistics, and actionable writing tips based on common email best practices.

What Gets Improved

  • Clarity — Removes vague language and tightens sentences
  • Tone — Ensures professional, appropriate phrasing
  • Structure — Improves paragraph flow and readability
  • Conciseness — Cuts unnecessary words and filler

Privacy-First AI

Your email content is never sent to any server. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware, ensuring complete privacy for sensitive business communications.
