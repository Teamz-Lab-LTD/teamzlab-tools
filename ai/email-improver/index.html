How the Email Improver Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Rewriter API to analyze your email draft and produce an improved version. The AI enhances clarity, fixes awkward phrasing, improves tone, and tightens structure while preserving your original message. In fallback mode, the tool provides word count, sentence statistics, and actionable writing tips based on common email best practices. Paste any email draft and get results instantly, whether it is a business proposal, a follow-up, or a client reply.

What Gets Improved in Your Email

The email improver targets four key areas that separate good emails from great ones. First, it removes vague language and tightens sentences for clarity. Second, it ensures professional and appropriate phrasing so your tone matches the context. Third, it improves paragraph flow and readability by restructuring awkward passages. Fourth, it cuts unnecessary filler words and redundant phrases to keep your message concise. Short, clear emails get faster responses and higher engagement rates than lengthy, unfocused ones.

Privacy-First Email Enhancement

Your email content is never sent to any server. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware, ensuring complete privacy for sensitive business communications. Unlike cloud-based writing assistants that store and process your text remotely, this tool keeps everything local. Whether you are drafting confidential client communications, internal reports, or personal messages, your words stay on your device. The fallback analysis mode also runs entirely in JavaScript within your browser.
