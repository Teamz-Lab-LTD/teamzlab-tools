How This Interview Question Generator Works

This tool generates 10 tailored interview questions based on the role, industry, seniority level, and focus area you specify. When Chrome AI is available, it uses the Prompt API to create custom questions. In all other browsers, it draws from a curated bank of 200+ questions categorized by type and difficulty.

Each question comes with a suggested answer framework — including STAR method guidance for behavioral questions — so you can prepare structured, compelling responses.

The STAR Method

  • Situation — Set the scene and provide context
  • Task — Describe your responsibility or challenge
  • Action — Explain the specific steps you took
  • Result — Share the outcome with measurable impact

Privacy-First AI

Your interview preparation data stays completely private. Chrome's Gemini Nano AI model runs locally on your hardware, and the question bank fallback processes everything in your browser. No data is transmitted to any server.
