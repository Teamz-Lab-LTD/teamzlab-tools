How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Summarizer API with the TL;DR type and short length to produce a concise summary of any text. The AI reads your entire input and distills it into a brief, accurate summary — typically 1-3 sentences.

If Chrome AI is not available, the tool extracts the first 2-3 sentences of your text as a basic summary, along with word count and reading time statistics.

Great For Summarizing

  • Articles — Get the gist of long blog posts and news articles
  • Email threads — Catch up on long email chains quickly
  • Documents — Summarize reports, proposals, and whitepapers
  • Research — Quick summaries of academic papers and studies

Privacy-First AI

Your text is processed entirely on your device — nothing is uploaded to any server. Whether you are summarizing confidential business documents or personal content, it stays private. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware.
