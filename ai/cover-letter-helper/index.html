How This AI Cover Letter Helper Works

This tool takes your job details — title, company, skills, experience, and preferred tone — and generates a complete cover letter draft. When Chrome AI is available, it uses the built-in Writer API to create a personalized letter. In all other browsers, it uses proven professional templates adapted to your inputs.

Every cover letter includes a compelling opening, a skills-focused body paragraph, and a confident closing — the three essential sections hiring managers look for.

Cover Letter Best Practices

  • Personalization — Address the specific company and role
  • Relevance — Highlight skills that match the job description
  • Conciseness — Keep it to one page, 3-4 paragraphs
  • Action Words — Use strong verbs like "led", "built", "improved"
  • Specific Results — Quantify achievements when possible

Privacy-First AI

Your career information stays on your device. Chrome's Gemini Nano AI model runs locally on your hardware, and the template fallback processes everything in your browser's JavaScript engine. No data is ever transmitted to any server.
