How the AI Email Writer Works

This tool generates complete, professional emails from your input using two methods. When Chrome's built-in Writer AI is available, it creates personalized emails using Gemini Nano — a language model that runs entirely on your device. Your email content, context, and personal details never leave your computer. When Chrome AI is not available, the tool uses structured professional email templates combined with your specific input to generate well-formatted emails for any business purpose.

Select your email purpose from 15 common categories, choose your preferred tone, enter your key points, and the tool produces a complete email with subject line, greeting, body, and closing. Each generated email is unique and tailored to your specific context and requirements.

Email Writing for Business Professionals

Business email writing follows established conventions that this tool handles automatically. Professional emails need a clear subject line that summarizes the purpose, an appropriate greeting based on formality level, a concise body that states the purpose early, and a call to action before the closing. The average professional sends 40 emails per day and receives over 100. Well-written emails that are clear and scannable get faster responses and better outcomes.

This tool covers the most common business email scenarios: meeting requests, follow-ups, proposals, introductions, complaints, and networking outreach. Each template follows best practices for its category — follow-up emails reference the original conversation, meeting requests include suggested times, and cold outreach emails lead with value rather than requests.

AI Email Templates and Customization

The fallback template system uses proven email structures for each purpose type. Business inquiry emails open with context and close with a specific ask. Thank-you emails acknowledge the specific action being appreciated. Resignation emails maintain professionalism while being direct about the decision. You can customize every generated email by editing the output directly or regenerating with different key points.

The tone selector adjusts vocabulary, sentence length, and formality level. Professional tone uses standard business language, friendly tone adds warmth while staying appropriate, formal tone uses traditional business conventions, and casual tone is suitable for internal team communications. Persuasive tone emphasizes benefits and includes subtle calls to action.

Privacy-First Email Generation

Email content often contains sensitive business information — client names, financial details, strategic plans, and personal data. Cloud-based email writers upload this content to remote servers for processing. This tool keeps everything local. Chrome's Writer API processes your request on-device, and the template fallback runs entirely in JavaScript within your browser. No accounts, no logins, no data collection. Your business communications remain completely confidential.
