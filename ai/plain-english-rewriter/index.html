How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Rewriter API to transform complex text into clear, everyday English. The AI preserves the original meaning while replacing jargon, simplifying sentence structure, and making the content accessible to a wider audience.

If Chrome AI is not available, the tool calculates a readability score (Flesch-Kincaid) and provides tips for simplifying the text — identifying long sentences, complex words, and passive voice.

What Gets Simplified

  • Legal jargon — "hereinafter" becomes "from now on", "notwithstanding" becomes "despite"
  • Technical terms — Replaced with everyday equivalents where possible
  • Long sentences — Broken into shorter, clearer statements
  • Passive voice — Converted to active voice for clarity

Privacy-First AI

Whether you are simplifying legal contracts, medical documents, or technical manuals, your text never leaves your browser. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware for complete privacy.
