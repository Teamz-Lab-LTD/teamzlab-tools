How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Rewriter API with a tone parameter to transform your text. The AI rewrites your content while preserving the original meaning, adjusting vocabulary, sentence structure, and phrasing to match your chosen tone.

If Chrome AI is not available, the tool applies rule-based word replacements — swapping formal words for casual equivalents (or vice versa) to approximate the desired tone.

Tone Options

  • Formal — Professional language suitable for business emails, reports, and official documents
  • Casual — Relaxed, everyday language for messages, social posts, and informal writing
  • Friendly — Warm, approachable tone for customer communication and team messages

Privacy-First AI

Unlike cloud-based rewriting tools, this processes everything locally. Your text never leaves your browser. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware for complete privacy.
