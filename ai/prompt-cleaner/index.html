Better prompts lead to better AI outputs. This tool helps you write prompts that are clear, specific, well-structured, and optimized for whatever AI model you are using.

Anatomy of a Great Prompt

  • Role/Persona — Tell the AI who to be ("You are an expert...")
  • Clear Instruction — Specific action verb ("Write", "Analyze", "Compare")
  • Context — Background information the AI needs
  • Output Format — How you want the response ("as a table", "in bullet points")
  • Constraints — Length, tone, style, what to avoid
  • Examples — Show the AI what good output looks like
