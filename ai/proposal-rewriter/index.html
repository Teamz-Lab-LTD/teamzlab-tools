How the Proposal Rewriter Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Rewriter API with the more-formal tone setting to transform your freelance proposal into a polished, professional version. The AI improves vocabulary, sentence structure, and overall persuasiveness while keeping your original message intact. When Chrome AI is unavailable, the tool provides rule-based word upgrade suggestions, proposal length analysis, and actionable tips based on proven freelance proposal best practices. It checks whether your proposal mentions experience, timelines, and calls to action.

What Gets Improved in Your Proposal

The rewriter targets four key areas that separate winning proposals from rejected ones. Professional tone upgrades casual language like "I can do" to confident phrasing like "I will deliver." Clarity improvements turn vague statements into specific, concrete commitments. Persuasiveness enhancements strengthen your value proposition and add compelling calls to action. Structure improvements ensure your proposal flows logically from greeting through experience to closing. Proposals between 150 and 300 words with specific references to the client's project consistently outperform generic submissions.

Private Proposal Enhancement

Your proposal content is never sent to any server. Whether you are bidding on Upwork, Fiverr, or responding to a client RFP, your text stays completely private on your device. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware. The word suggestion engine and proposal analysis also run entirely in JavaScript within your browser. This protects your pricing strategy, client relationships, and competitive positioning from being exposed to third-party services.
