How This AI Headline Generator Works

This tool generates 10 compelling headline variations based on your topic, tone, and content type. When Chrome AI is available, it uses the built-in Writer API. In all other browsers, it applies proven copywriting formulas used by top marketers and journalists.

Each headline shows character count (important for SEO titles and email subject lines) and an emotional word score that estimates how engaging the headline will be.

Headline Formulas Used

  • How-To — "How to [Action] Without [Pain Point]"
  • Listicle — "[Number] Ways to [Benefit] in [Timeframe]"
  • Ultimate Guide — "The Ultimate Guide to [Topic]"
  • Curiosity Gap — "Why [Surprising Fact] About [Topic]"
  • Mistake Avoidance — "[Number] [Topic] Mistakes You Are Making"
