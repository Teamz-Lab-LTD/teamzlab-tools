How the Social Media Post Generator Works

The social media post generator creates platform-optimized content based on your topic, chosen platform, and desired tone. When Chrome AI is available, the tool uses on-device AI to craft original posts tailored to each platform's best practices. In all other browsers, a Transformers.js model or a template engine generates posts using proven social media copywriting formulas. Each post includes relevant hashtags, calls to action, and emoji usage appropriate for the platform. Character counts are displayed so you can verify the post fits platform limits before publishing.

Platform-Specific Post Optimization

Instagram posts work best at 150 to 2200 characters with 8 to 15 hashtags in a separate block. Facebook posts perform best under 250 characters for maximum engagement but can be longer for storytelling. LinkedIn posts should be professional and value-driven, using 1300 characters or less with 3 to 5 hashtags. X/Twitter posts must fit within 280 characters with 1 to 2 hashtags woven into the text. TikTok captions should be short and punchy under 150 characters with 3 to 5 trending hashtags. The generator automatically adjusts length, tone, and formatting for each platform.
