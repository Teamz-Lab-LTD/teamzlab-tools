How This AI Tool Works

This tool uses Chrome's built-in Summarizer API with the key-points type to extract the most important items from your text and present them as a checklist. The AI understands context and groups related items intelligently.

If Chrome AI is not available, the tool splits text on sentence boundaries and creates a checklist item for each distinct point. It also detects existing bullet points and numbered lists.

Great For

  • Instructions — Turn written steps into a step-by-step checklist
  • Requirements — Extract requirements from a paragraph into checkable items
  • Meeting notes — Pull action items into a to-do list
  • Recipes — Extract ingredient lists or cooking steps

Privacy-First AI

Your text is processed entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Chrome runs the Gemini Nano AI model directly on your hardware for complete privacy.
