Your Complete Outdoor Toolkit

Whether you are planning a weekend car camping trip or preparing for a multi-day backcountry expedition, these tools help you prepare smarter and stay safer. Every tool runs entirely in your browser with no data sent to any server.

Planning Tools

Start with the Camping Checklist to make sure you pack everything you need. Use the Camping Meal Planner to build a complete food plan with shopping lists. The Backpack Weight Calculator helps ultralight hikers optimize every ounce.

Survival Skills

The Survival Kit Builder walks you through assembling a proper emergency kit. Learn essential knots, water purification methods and fire-starting techniques with our interactive guides. These references work offline once loaded — perfect for actual wilderness use.

Navigation and Timing

Use the Online Compass on your phone for quick orientation checks. The Sunrise Sunset Calculator gives you precise daylight hours, golden hour times for photography, and twilight periods for any location on Earth.

Fitness and Nutrition

The Hiking Calorie Calculator estimates your energy expenditure based on terrain, elevation, pack weight and body weight. Use the results to plan how much food and water to bring on your next hike.
