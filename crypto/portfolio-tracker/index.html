How to Track Your Crypto Portfolio for Free

This free crypto portfolio tracker lets you monitor the combined value of your cryptocurrency holdings in real time — no account, no app download, and no data shared with any server. Select a coin from the dropdown, enter the amount you hold, and click "Add Coin." The tool immediately fetches the current price from the CoinGecko public API and calculates your holding's total value. Your portfolio is automatically saved to your browser's localStorage, so your holdings are still there when you return. Based on CoinGecko data. Last updated: March 2026.

You can track up to 15 popular cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), BNB, Cardano (ADA), XRP, Dogecoin (DOGE), Polkadot (DOT), Avalanche (AVAX), Chainlink (LINK), Litecoin (LTC), Stellar (XLM), Uniswap (UNI), Tron (TRX), and Shiba Inu (SHIB). Portfolio values can be displayed in USD, EUR, GBP, or AUD.

Live Bitcoin and Ethereum Prices

Prices are fetched directly from the CoinGecko public API — one of the most trusted crypto data sources, used by thousands of apps worldwide. The API provides real-time prices with 24-hour percentage changes, so you can see at a glance whether your holdings are up or down today. Green values indicate a price gain; muted values indicate a price drop. Prices auto-refresh every 5 minutes while the page is open, and you can also click "Refresh Prices" for an immediate update.

The 24-hour change percentage shown next to each coin lets you spot daily movers quickly. If Bitcoin is up 3% but Solana is down 2%, you can see the net effect on your total portfolio value at a glance. The total portfolio value at the top updates automatically whenever prices refresh.

Portfolio Tracker vs Crypto Exchange Apps

Exchange apps like Coinbase, Binance, and Kraken only show balances held on their own platform. If you hold coins across multiple wallets, hardware wallets (like Ledger), or different exchanges, you need a separate tracker to see your complete picture. This tool is exchange-agnostic — you simply enter the amounts you hold regardless of where they are stored.

Unlike dedicated portfolio apps such as CoinStats, Delta, or Blockfolio, this tracker requires no account, no email, and no app installation. It works in any browser, including on mobile. There are no premium tiers, no ads to sign up through, and no data collection. It is a pure browser tool.

Use the Export CSV button to download your portfolio as a spreadsheet. This makes it easy to import into Google Sheets, Excel, or your own tax software for further analysis.

Keeping Your Crypto Data Private

Privacy is built into the design. Your portfolio holdings — which coins you own and how much — are saved only in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device. The only external connection this tool makes is a price fetch from the CoinGecko API, which is a standard public price feed that does not receive any information about your holdings. CoinGecko's API call contains only the list of coin IDs you selected; it has no way to associate that request with a specific portfolio or user.

If you clear your browser data or use Private/Incognito mode, your saved portfolio will not persist. For a permanent record, use the Export CSV feature to save a snapshot to your own storage. You can re-import values manually at any time by adding coins again from the dropdown.
