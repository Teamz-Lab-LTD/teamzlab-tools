Payslip Components Explained

A standard payslip contains several sections that employees and employers need to understand. Earnings include the basic salary, which is the fixed monthly amount, plus variable components like housing allowance, transport allowance, and any bonuses or overtime pay. Deductions cover mandatory items like income tax and pension contributions, plus optional items like health insurance premiums and loan repayments. The net salary is what the employee actually receives after all deductions are subtracted from gross earnings.

Different countries have different requirements for payslip content. The UK requires employers to provide itemized payslips by law. The US requires pay stubs in most states. This generator covers the universal components that apply across jurisdictions, which you can customize with your specific line items.
