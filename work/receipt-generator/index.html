Receipt vs Invoice — What's the Difference?

A receipt is proof that payment has been received — it's given after money changes hands. An invoice is a request for payment — it's sent before payment is made. Use this receipt generator when you've already been paid. Need to request payment instead? Try our free invoice generator. Both tools are 100% browser-based and private.

Tips for Creating Professional Receipts

Always include a unique receipt number for easy tracking. Break down tax separately for legal compliance in most jurisdictions. Add your business logo to reinforce brand recognition. Include a "Thank you" note to encourage repeat business. Keep digital copies of all receipts for tax season — this tool lets you download as PDF or PNG for easy archiving.
