3D Tools

Free browser-based 3D tools for 3D printing, modeling, and design. View STL files, convert between 3D formats, estimate printing costs and time, generate 3D text, and create product mockups — all without installing software. Everything runs locally in your browser.

3D Model Viewer

View GLB, GLTF, OBJ, STL, FBX, 3DS, PLY files in your browser. Rotate, zoom, wireframe, screenshot — free Sketchfab alternative.

WebGL Fluid Simulation

Interactive fluid dynamics — click, drag, and create mesmerizing colorful patterns with real-time physics.

3D Audio Visualizer

Upload music and watch stunning 3D visuals react to the beat. Bars, sphere, waveform, and particle modes.

3D Dice Roller

Roll realistic 3D dice with physics — d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20 for D&D, board games, and RPGs.

3D Product Mockup Generator

Upload your label or logo, map it onto a 3D box, rotate the view, and download a product mockup as PNG.

3D Text Generator

Create 3D text with custom fonts, colors, and extrusion depth. Rotate and download as PNG.

STL Viewer

View STL files in your browser with 3D rotation, zoom, and wireframe mode. No software needed.

STL to OBJ Converter

Convert STL files to OBJ format instantly in your browser. Free, private, no upload to server.

3D Printing Cost Calculator

Estimate the material and electricity cost of a 3D print based on filament type, weight, and print time.

3D Print Time Estimator

Estimate how long your 3D print will take based on layer height, print speed, and model dimensions.
