Kids & Education Tools

Free, private tools for children's education and health. Math practice, spelling quizzes, reading tests, growth tracking, and more. No sign-up required.

Education Tools

Practice multiplication with timed quizzes, score tracking, streak counter, and review of wrong answers. Choose tables 2-12.

Find your reading grade level with progressively harder passages and comprehension questions. Grades 1-8 with fun badges.

Generate endless word problems by grade level. Addition, subtraction, multiplication, fractions, percentages with step-by-step solutions.

Fun typing speed test with WPM and accuracy tracking. Earn badges: Turtle, Rabbit, Cheetah, or Lightning based on your speed.

Handwriting Practice

Trace letters, numbers, and words on a canvas. Touch and mouse support. Dotted outlines guide each character.
