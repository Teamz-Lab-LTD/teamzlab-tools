Free Math Tools & Calculators

Free browser-based math calculators that show step-by-step solutions for every answer. Whether you are a student working through homework, a teacher preparing examples, or a professional double-checking calculations, these tools cover percentages, fractions, ratios, averages, standard deviation, permutations, combinations, probability, logarithms, matrices, and trigonometry. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server, no sign-up is needed, and nothing is stored. Just enter your numbers, hit calculate, and see the complete working. Perfect for exam preparation, quick verification, and building mathematical intuition.
