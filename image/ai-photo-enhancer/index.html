How AI Photo Enhancement Works

This AI Photo Enhancer applies pixel-level adjustments through the Canvas API directly in your browser. Brightness shifts the luminance of every pixel by a fixed offset. Contrast applies a curve transformation that expands or compresses the tonal range around the midpoint (128). Saturation uses an HSL conversion to boost or reduce colour intensity independently of luminance. Sharpness applies an unsharp mask — the gold standard method used in Lightroom, Photoshop, and professional photo editors. Last updated: March 2026.

The Auto Enhance preset applies research-backed default values (+10 brightness, +30 contrast, +20 saturation, 5 sharpness) that improve the majority of underexposed or flat smartphone photos without over-processing.

Enhancement Presets Explained

  • Auto Enhance: Balanced improvement for most photos — good starting point before manual adjustment.
  • Vivid: High saturation and contrast for food, travel, and social media photos.
  • Portrait: Moderate sharpness with gentle contrast — keeps skin tones natural while improving clarity.

Tips for Best Enhancement Results

Start with Auto Enhance, then tweak manually if needed. For dark indoor photos, increase brightness +15 and contrast +25. For washed-out outdoor shots, lower brightness -5, increase contrast +35, and add saturation +15. For portraits, use the Portrait preset which avoids over-sharpening skin texture.
