New Zealand Tools

Free online tools built for New Zealanders. Plan your KiwiSaver retirement savings, calculate employer contributions, and make smarter financial decisions. Everything runs in your browser — 100% private, no sign-up required.

From KiwiSaver retirement projections to ESCT-aware employer contribution checks and tax calculators, these tools are tailored to New Zealand's rules and rates. Built around IRD and Inland Revenue guidelines. All calculations happen client-side — no data ever leaves your device.

KiwiSaver Calculator

Calculate your KiwiSaver retirement balance, contributions, and employer match in seconds.

KiwiSaver Employer Contribution Calculator

Calculate gross employer contributions, ESCT, and the net amount landing in your KiwiSaver account.

NZ PAYE Tax Calculator

Calculate your New Zealand take-home pay after income tax, ACC levy, and KiwiSaver deductions.

NZ Mortgage Fix vs Float

Compare fixed and floating mortgage rates and find the lowest-cost option for your NZ home loan.

NZ First Home Grant Calculator

Calculate your First Home Grant and KiwiSaver withdrawal for buying your first home in New Zealand.

NZ Student Loan Repayment Calculator

Calculate your IRD student loan repayments, years to repay, and savings from voluntary extra payments. 0% interest for NZ residents.

NZ Rental Yield Calculator

Calculate gross and net rental yield on New Zealand investment properties. Compare returns across suburbs and property types.

NZ Bright-Line Tax Calculator

Estimate Bright-Line property tax on NZ residential property sales under the 2-year rule from 1 July 2024.
