## Quota Management Rules

- **NEVER** schedule heavy Claude agents during break or sleep — wastes quota
- **Overnight automation = bash/Python scripts ONLY** (zero Claude quota)
- **Batch heavy builds right after quota resets** — not at end of day
- **Use Python scripts** for bulk page generation (programmatic SEO, salary cities)
- **Use Claude agents** only for unique tool creation and research
- **Max 2-3 parallel agents** to avoid hitting limits
