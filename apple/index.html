Tools for Everyday Apple Problems

Apple devices are easy to use most of the time, but the small planning and troubleshooting questions add up quickly. How much iPhone storage do you actually need? Which iCloud plan makes sense? Is your battery drain normal? Should you trade in a device or keep it longer? This page groups those common questions into simple browser tools with no account required.

The collection covers iPhone, iPad, Mac, iCloud, AirDrop, storage planning, battery health, screen-time habits, media file size, and Apple event timing. Instead of searching through scattered forum threads or rough spreadsheets, you can use focused tools that explain the decision and give you a fast answer. That is especially useful when you are comparing device options, preparing for an upgrade, or helping family members manage their Apple setup.
