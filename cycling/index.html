Your Complete Cycling Toolkit

Whether you are a daily bike commuter, a weekend warrior or just getting into cycling, these free tools help you ride smarter and save money. Every tool runs entirely in your browser with no data sent to any server.

Bike Fit and Setup

Start with the Bike Size Calculator to find your ideal frame size based on your height and inseam measurements. Then use the Bike Tire Pressure Calculator to dial in the perfect PSI for your weight, tire width and riding conditions. Proper setup means more comfort and fewer flats.

Fitness and Savings

The Cycling Calories Calculator estimates your energy burn based on speed, distance, weight and terrain type. Track your fitness progress over time. The Bike Commute Savings Calculator shows exactly how much money you save by cycling instead of driving, factoring in fuel, parking, insurance and maintenance costs.

Electric Bikes and Maintenance

Planning an e-bike purchase or ride? The E-Bike Range Calculator estimates how far you can go on a single charge based on battery capacity, rider weight, terrain and assist level. Keep your bike running smoothly with the Bicycle Maintenance Checklist, an interactive tracker covering daily, weekly and monthly service tasks.
