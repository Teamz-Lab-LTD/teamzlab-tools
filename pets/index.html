Free Pet Tools for Every Pet Owner

Whether you just brought home a new puppy or have been a lifelong cat parent, our pet tools help you take better care of your furry friends. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — your pet's data never leaves your device.

Calculators

Our Dog Age Calculator uses the 2019 UCSD epigenetic study formula, not the outdated 7x rule. The Cat Age Calculator follows veterinary guidelines adjusted for indoor vs outdoor cats. The Pet Food Calculator uses resting energy requirements to give you precise daily portions.

Planning Tools

Thinking about getting a pet? Take the Dog Breed Quiz to find your ideal match, then use the Pet Cost Calculator to plan your budget. Already have a pet? Track their health with the Pet Weight Tracker and celebrate milestones with the Pet Birthday Calculator.

Safety First

The Toxic Food Checker covers 100+ foods with specific information for both dogs and cats. The Pet First Aid Guide provides step-by-step emergency instructions. Bookmark both — you never know when you'll need them.
