What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It originates from "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" by Cicero, written in 45 BC. The text has a natural distribution of letter frequencies and word lengths, making it look like real prose without distracting readers with meaningful content. This allows designers and developers to evaluate typography, spacing, and layout independently from final copy.

How the Lorem Ipsum Generator Works

Choose your output type (paragraphs, sentences, or words), set the quantity, and optionally start with the classic "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" opening. The generator draws from a curated Latin word pool and assembles text with randomized sentence lengths between 8 and 19 words. Each sentence is capitalized and terminated with a period. Paragraphs contain 4 to 7 sentences, mimicking the rhythm of natural prose. The word and character counts are displayed so you can match specific design requirements.

When to Use Placeholder Text

Lorem Ipsum is essential during wireframing, prototyping, and UI development when final content is unavailable. Use it to test how text wraps inside containers, verify font sizes across breakpoints, and check that layouts handle varying content lengths gracefully. It is also useful for populating database seed data during development. For user testing, consider switching to realistic content early, as Lorem Ipsum can mask readability issues and content hierarchy problems that only surface with real words.
