2025-26 Champions League Format

The 2025-26 UCL uses the new Swiss-system league phase format introduced in 2024-25. Thirty-six teams play 8 matches each in the league phase, with the top 8 qualifying directly for the Round of 16 and teams ranked 9th to 24th entering a playoff round. The knockout stage then follows the traditional two-leg format through to the Semi-finals, with the Final being a single match.

The Final in Munich

The 2025-26 UEFA Champions League Final will be held at the Allianz Arena in Munich, Germany on Saturday, May 30, 2026. Munich is one of European football's great cities, home to Bayern Munich and a venue with a rich history in the competition. The Allianz Arena, with a capacity of approximately 70,000, will provide a spectacular setting for European club football's biggest match.
