About the UCL Quiz

The UEFA Champions League is the most prestigious club football competition in the world. Since its inception as the European Cup in 1955, it has produced countless iconic moments, legendary players, and unforgettable matches. This quiz tests your knowledge across the entire history of the competition.

Questions cover a wide range of topics including record holders, historic finals, famous goals, team achievements, and tournament milestones. Whether you have been following the Champions League for decades or are a newer fan, you will find questions that challenge and educate you.

How the Scoring Works

You earn one point for each correct answer, giving a maximum score of 20. Your time per question is tracked but does not affect your score — however, unanswered questions when time runs out count as incorrect. At the end, you receive a rating based on your score: Legend (18-20), Expert (14-17), Fan (10-13), Casual (6-9), or Beginner (0-5).

Champions League Records to Know

Real Madrid holds the record for most UCL titles with 15. Cristiano Ronaldo is the all-time top scorer with 140 goals. The competition has been won by clubs from Spain, England, Italy, Germany, Netherlands, Portugal, Scotland, Romania, Serbia, and France. The final has been held at iconic venues across Europe, from Wembley to the Santiago Bernabeu.
