App Store and Play Store Screenshot Size Guide

Submitting an app to the Apple App Store or Google Play Store requires providing screenshots in specific dimensions. Apple mandates screenshots for the 6.7-inch iPhone display and the 12.9-inch iPad display if your app supports iPad. Google Play requires between two and eight phone screenshots plus a mandatory feature graphic. Getting these dimensions wrong leads to rejected submissions and delayed launches, which is why having a reliable reference matters.

Key Sizing Rules

Apple App Store: You must provide screenshots for the 6.7-inch iPhone (1290 x 2796 px portrait). If your app runs on iPad, the 12.9-inch iPad (2048 x 2732 px portrait) is also required. You can upload up to 10 screenshots per device size. Google Play Store: Phone screenshots must be between 320 px and 3840 px on any side with a 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio. The feature graphic (1024 x 500 px) is required for all apps.

Why Screenshot Sizes Matter for App Store Optimization

Screenshots are the most influential factor in app store conversion rates after the app icon. Research shows that up to 60 percent of users make their download decision without scrolling past the screenshot gallery. Providing crisp, correctly sized screenshots ensures they render without scaling artifacts and look professional on every device. Blurry or improperly cropped screenshots signal low quality and reduce install rates.

Apple App Store Requirements

Apple requires screenshots in PNG or JPEG format without alpha transparency. The 6.7-inch display size (iPhone 15 Pro Max and iPhone 16 Pro Max) serves as the primary required size. If you only upload one set, Apple will scale these screenshots for smaller iPhone displays. For iPad apps, the 12.9-inch iPad Pro screenshots are required and cannot be auto-generated from iPhone screenshots. You can upload between 2 and 10 screenshots per localization per device.

Google Play Store Requirements

Google Play accepts JPEG or 24-bit PNG screenshots with sides between 320 and 3840 pixels. The aspect ratio must not exceed 2:1 in either direction. You need a minimum of two and can upload up to eight phone screenshots. The feature graphic at 1024 x 500 pixels is mandatory and appears prominently at the top of your store listing. If you have a promo video, the thumbnail at 1280 x 720 pixels is recommended. Tablet, Wear OS, and TV screenshots are optional but recommended if your app supports those form factors.

Best Practices for Screenshot Creation

Design screenshots at the largest required size first, then scale down for smaller devices. For Apple, start with the 6.7-inch and 12.9-inch sizes. Use portrait orientation for most apps and landscape for games. Include short, benefit-focused captions above or below the device frame. Keep text large enough to read on the store listing grid view. Test how your screenshots look both in search results and on the full listing page, as they render at different sizes in each context.
