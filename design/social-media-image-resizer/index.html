Social Media Image Sizes Guide

Every social media platform has recommended image dimensions. Using the wrong size leads to cropping, blurring, or awkward framing. This social media image resizer includes all current recommended sizes for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, and TikTok. Upload once, resize for every platform.

Key Image Sizes (2026)

Instagram Post: 1080 × 1080 (1:1 square)

Instagram Story: 1080 × 1920 (9:16)

Facebook Cover: 1640 × 624

Twitter/X Header: 1500 × 500 (3:1)

YouTube Thumbnail: 1280 × 720 (16:9)

LinkedIn Banner: 1584 × 396

Why Image Size Matters for Social Media

Social platforms compress and crop images that do not match their recommended dimensions. An image that looks great on your phone may appear pixelated or cut off when posted. Using the correct dimensions ensures your content looks professional and takes up maximum screen real estate in feeds.

How This Tool Works
