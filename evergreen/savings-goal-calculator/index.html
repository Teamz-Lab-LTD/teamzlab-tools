How to Calculate Monthly Savings

This calculator works backward from your goal: given a target amount, time frame, initial savings, and expected return rate, it computes the monthly contribution needed. It uses the future value of annuity formula solved for the payment amount.

Monthly Savings Formula

PMT = (Goal − P(1+r)ⁿ) × r / ((1+r)ⁿ − 1)

Where: Goal = target amount, P = current savings, r = monthly rate, n = months

Example

$50,000 down payment in 5 years, $5,000 saved, 4% return
