How Sales Tax Is Calculated

Sales tax is a percentage added to the purchase price at the point of sale. The rate varies by state, county, and city in the US, and by country internationally. This calculator lets you enter any rate and works in both directions — add tax to a pre-tax price, or remove tax to find the original price.

Sales Tax Formulas

Tax Amount = Price × (Tax Rate / 100)

Total with Tax = Price + Tax Amount

Pre-Tax Price = Total / (1 + Tax Rate / 100)
