Cost of Living in Dublin

Dublin is Europe's tech hub hosting Apple, Google, and Meta's European HQs. High housing costs but competitive salaries. The city has a cost of living index of 73 relative to New York City (100), making it 27% cheaper than New York. Average salaries in Dublin are around €50,000 per year, though this varies significantly by industry and experience level.

Housing is typically the largest expense, accounting for 35-40% of total living costs. In Dublin, housing costs are above the global city average. Food, transportation, and healthcare make up most of the remaining budget.

Working and Living in Dublin

Dublin offers a high cost of living but strong career opportunities for professionals. The city's job market is competitive but rewarding.
