How to Convert Hourly Rate to Salary

To convert an hourly rate to an annual salary, multiply the hourly rate by the number of hours worked per week, then by the number of weeks worked per year. The standard assumption is 40 hours per week and 52 weeks per year (2,080 hours), but this calculator lets you customize both values for more accurate results.

Conversion Formulas

Annual Salary = Hourly Rate × Hours/Week × Weeks/Year

Monthly Pay = Annual Salary / 12

Biweekly Pay = Hourly Rate × Hours/Week × 2

Weekly Pay = Hourly Rate × Hours/Week

Daily Pay = Hourly Rate × Hours/Day

Example Calculations

Example 1: $25/hour Full-Time

$25/hr × 40 hrs/week × 52 weeks = $52,000/year ($4,333/month)

Example 2: $18/hour Part-Time (30 hrs/week)

$18/hr × 30 hrs/week × 50 weeks = $27,000/year ($2,250/month)
