Understanding Rent Increases

Rent increases are typically applied annually as a percentage of current rent. The national average rent increase in the US is around 3-5% per year, though this varies widely by city and market conditions. Some jurisdictions have rent control laws that cap increases. This calculator helps you understand the exact dollar impact of any proposed increase.

Rent Increase Formula

New Rent = Current Rent × (1 + Increase % / 100)

Monthly Increase = New Rent − Current Rent

Annual Impact = Monthly Increase × 12
