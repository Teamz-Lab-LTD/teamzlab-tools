What Is a QR Code?

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that store data readable by cameras and QR scanner apps. They can encode URLs, text, WiFi credentials, contact information, email addresses, phone numbers, and more. QR codes are used on product packaging, business cards, restaurant menus, event tickets, and marketing materials.

QR Code Types

URL: paste any web address. WiFi: use format WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;; Email: mailto:user@example.com. Phone: tel:+1234567890. SMS: smsto:+1234567890:message. Plain text: any text up to ~4,296 characters.
