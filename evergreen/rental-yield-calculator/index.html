What Is Rental Yield?

Rental yield measures the annual rental income as a percentage of the property's value. Gross yield uses total rent; net yield subtracts expenses. A good rental yield is typically 5-8% gross, though this varies by market.

Rental Yield Formulas

Gross Yield = (Annual Rent / Property Price) × 100

Net Yield = ((Annual Rent − Annual Expenses) / Property Price) × 100
