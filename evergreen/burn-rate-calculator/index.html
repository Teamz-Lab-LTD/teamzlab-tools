How Burn Rate Works

Burn rate measures how quickly a company spends its cash reserves. Gross burn rate is total monthly spending. Net burn rate is spending minus revenue — the actual cash loss per month. For startups, burn rate is the most critical metric alongside runway because it determines how long you can operate before needing more funding or reaching profitability.

Burn Rate Formulas

Gross Burn Rate = Total Monthly Expenses

Net Burn Rate = Monthly Expenses − Monthly Revenue

Runway = Cash Balance / Net Burn Rate
