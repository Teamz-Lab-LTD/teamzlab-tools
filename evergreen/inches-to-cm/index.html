How to Convert Inches to Centimeters

Converting inches to centimeters is straightforward. One inch equals exactly 2.54 centimeters. This relationship was standardized internationally in 1959. To convert any measurement from inches to centimeters, simply multiply the number of inches by 2.54. For example, 10 inches equals 25.4 cm (10 × 2.54 = 25.4).

Conversion Formulas

Inches to CM: cm = inches × 2.54

CM to Inches: inches = cm ÷ 2.54

How to Convert CM to Inches

To convert centimeters to inches, divide the centimeter value by 2.54. For example, 50 cm = 50 ÷ 2.54 = 19.69 inches. This is useful when you have metric measurements and need imperial equivalents — common when shopping for screens, furniture, or clothing across different regions.

Common Uses for Inches to CM Conversion

Inches-to-centimeter conversion comes up frequently in everyday life. Screen sizes for TVs, monitors, and phones are typically measured in inches. Height measurements use feet and inches in the US but centimeters in most other countries. Clothing sizes, paper dimensions, and DIY projects often require switching between the two systems. A quick reference: 1 foot (12 inches) equals 30.48 cm, and 1 meter (100 cm) equals about 39.37 inches.
