APR vs Interest Rate

The interest rate is the cost of borrowing the principal. APR includes the interest rate PLUS fees, closing costs, and other charges, spread over the loan term. Two loans with the same interest rate can have different APRs if one has higher fees. APR gives you the true cost of borrowing.

APR Calculation

Effective Loan = Loan Amount − Total Fees

APR = Rate that makes monthly payment on (Loan Amount) equal to monthly payment on (Effective Loan at nominal rate)

This calculator uses an iterative approximation method.
