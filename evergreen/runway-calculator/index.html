How Runway Is Calculated

Runway is the number of months a company can continue operating at its current burn rate before running out of cash. It is the most critical planning metric for startups and any business managing limited cash reserves. Investors typically want to see 12-18 months of runway when evaluating a company.

Runway Formula

Runway (months) = Cash Balance / Net Monthly Burn

Net Monthly Burn = Monthly Expenses − Monthly Revenue

Cash-Out Date = Today + Runway months
