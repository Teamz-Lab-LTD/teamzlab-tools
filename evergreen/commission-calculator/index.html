How Commission Is Calculated

Sales commission is a percentage of revenue paid to the salesperson. The basic formula is straightforward: commission = sales × rate. Many compensation plans also include a base salary on top of commission, creating a total compensation package.

Commission Formula

Commission = Sales Amount × (Commission Rate / 100)

Total Earnings = Base Salary + Commission

Effective Rate = (Commission / Sales) × 100

Example

$150,000 in sales, 8% commission, $3,000 base

  • Commission: $12,000 | Total: $15,000
