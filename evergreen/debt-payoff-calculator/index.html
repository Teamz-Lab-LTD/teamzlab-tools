How Debt Payoff Is Calculated

This calculator simulates monthly payments on a balance with interest. Each month, interest accrues on the remaining balance, and your payment covers the interest plus some principal. The simulation runs until the balance reaches zero, giving you the exact payoff timeline and total interest cost.

Payoff Formula

Monthly Interest = Balance × (Annual Rate / 12)

Principal Payment = Monthly Payment − Monthly Interest

New Balance = Old Balance − Principal Payment

Months to Payoff = solve iteratively until balance = 0
