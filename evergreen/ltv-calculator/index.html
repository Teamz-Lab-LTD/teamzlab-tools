How LTV Is Calculated

Customer Lifetime Value (LTV or CLV) estimates the total revenue you can expect from a single customer over the duration of their relationship. The simplest formula multiplies average revenue per customer by average customer lifespan. More advanced versions factor in gross margin and churn rate.

LTV Formulas

Simple LTV = ARPU × Average Customer Lifespan (months)

LTV with Margin = ARPU × Gross Margin % × Lifespan

LTV from Churn = ARPU × Gross Margin / Monthly Churn Rate

Average Lifespan = 1 / Monthly Churn Rate

Example

SaaS Subscription

ARPU: $99/month, Gross margin: 80%, Monthly churn: 3%

  • Lifespan: 1 / 0.03 = 33.3 months
  • LTV: $99 × 0.80 × 33.3 = $2,640
