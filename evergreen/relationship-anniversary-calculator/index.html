How the Anniversary Calculator Works

Enter the date you started your relationship and instantly see exactly how long you have been together. The calculator shows years, months, days, hours, minutes, and fun stats like total heartbeats and sunsets shared. It also lists upcoming milestones so you never miss an anniversary.

Fun Relationship Stats

Heartbeats: ~100,000 beats per day × days together

Sunsets: One per day you have been together

Meals: ~3 per day × days together

Sleep: ~8 hours × days = hours slept beside each other

Relationship Milestones to Celebrate

Beyond yearly anniversaries, celebrate unique milestones: your 100th day together, 500 days, 1000 days, 10,000 hours, and more. These micro-celebrations keep the romance alive and give you reasons to appreciate your journey together.

Anniversary Gift Ideas by Year

Traditional anniversary gifts follow a pattern: 1st year — paper, 2nd — cotton, 3rd — leather, 5th — wood, 10th — tin, 15th — crystal, 20th — china, 25th — silver, 50th — gold. Modern alternatives are more flexible, but knowing the tradition adds a thoughtful touch to your gift.

Why Tracking Your Anniversary Matters

Remembering and celebrating your anniversary shows your partner that you value the relationship. It is a dedicated moment to reflect on your journey, appreciate growth, and recommit to each other. Even a simple acknowledgment — a note, a favorite meal, or a walk together — can mean the world.
