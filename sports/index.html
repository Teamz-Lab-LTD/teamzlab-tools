Sports & Fitness Calculators

Calculate running pace, calories burned, BMI, body fat, heart rate zones, VO2 max, one-rep max, and more. All tools run client-side in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

Running Pace Calculator

Calculate running pace in min/km or min/mi, speed, and predict finish times for 5K, 10K, half marathon, and marathon.

Calories Burned Calculator

Estimate calories burned during running, walking, cycling, swimming, HIIT, and 12+ activities using MET values.

BMI Calculator

Calculate Body Mass Index with health category, healthy weight range, and BMI Prime. Supports metric and imperial units.

Body Fat Estimator

Estimate body fat percentage using the US Navy circumference method. Shows fat mass, lean mass, and fitness category.

Ideal Weight Calculator

Find your ideal weight range using Devine, Robinson, Miller, and Hamwi formulas with body frame adjustment.

Heart Rate Zone Calculator

Calculate your five training heart rate zones using Max HR or Karvonen method. Find zones for fat burn and endurance.

VO2 Max Estimator

Estimate your VO2 max using Cooper Test, 1.5 Mile Run, or Rockport Walk Test. Get fitness rating by age and gender.

Race Finish Time Predictor

Predict finish times for 5K, 10K, 15K, half marathon, and marathon using the Riegel formula from a known race result.

Split Time Calculator

Generate even-split pacing tables for any race distance with cumulative time and pace per segment.

One Rep Max (1RM) Calculator

Estimate your one-rep max using Epley, Brzycki, Lombardi, and O'Conner formulas. Get training weight percentages.

Marathon Wall Predictor

Predict if and where you will hit the wall during a marathon. Analyzes glycogen, pace, training, and carb loading.

Cricket Run Rate Calculator

Calculate required run rate, projected score, and chase difficulty for ODI, T20, and custom cricket formats.
