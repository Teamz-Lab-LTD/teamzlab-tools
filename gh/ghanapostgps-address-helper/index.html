What Is GhanaPostGPS?

GhanaPostGPS is a digital addressing system developed by the Ghana Post and Vokacom Limited. It assigns a unique digital address to every five-metre square area in Ghana. The system uses a combination of letters and numbers in the format XX-123-4567, where the first two letters represent the region/area code and the numbers identify the specific location. This digital address can be used as a precise location identifier for deliveries, emergency services, government services, banking, and navigation. The system was launched in 2017 and has become increasingly important for formal transactions and service delivery in Ghana.

How to Find Your GhanaPostGPS Code

There are several ways to find your GhanaPostGPS code. The easiest method is to download the GhanaPostGPS mobile app (available on both Android and iOS) and generate your address while standing at your location. The app uses your phone's GPS to determine your exact position and assigns the corresponding digital address. You can also visit the GhanaPostGPS website at ghanapostgps.com and use the map to find your location. Additionally, you can visit any Ghana Post office and request assistance in generating your digital address. Once you have your code, save it in your phone contacts and share it with anyone who needs to find your location.

GhanaPostGPS Code Format

Format: XX-123-4567

  • XX = Region/Area prefix (e.g., GA for Greater Accra, AK for Ashanti)
  • 123 = Area identifier within the region
  • 4567 = Specific 5m×5m location

Where to Use Your GhanaPostGPS Address

Your GhanaPostGPS digital address is increasingly required or useful in many situations. For deliveries, share your GPS code with Jumia, Glovo, Bolt Food, or any delivery service for precise location. For ride-hailing, enter your GPS code in Bolt or Uber for exact pickup and drop-off. For official forms, many government agencies now require a digital address on applications. For banking, the Bank of Ghana requires digital addresses for KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance. For emergency services, sharing your GPS code helps ambulance and fire services find your exact location quickly. For business registration, the Registrar General's Department requires a digital address for company registration.

Region Codes in GhanaPostGPS

Each of Ghana's 16 regions has unique prefix codes in the GhanaPostGPS system. Greater Accra uses codes starting with GA, Ashanti uses AK, Western uses WS/WR, Eastern uses ER, Central uses CC, Northern uses NT, Volta uses VT, Upper East uses UE, Upper West uses UW, Bono uses BA, Ahafo uses AF, Savannah uses SV, Oti uses OT, North East uses NR, Western North uses WN, and Bono East uses BE. Knowing your region prefix helps verify that your digital address is correct for your area.
