Free Online Calculators & Tools

Instant, no-login tools for work transitions, invoicing, and freelancing. Built by Teamz Lab.

Whether you are resigning from a job, sending an invoice, or setting your freelance rates, these calculators give you instant, accurate answers without sign-ups or downloads. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

We cover work transitions (notice periods, leave encashment, probation dates, salary breakdowns), invoicing (due dates, late fees, payment terms), and freelance business (hourly rates, project pricing, retainers, profit margins). All tools include formula explanations, worked examples, and FAQs so you understand how the math works.
