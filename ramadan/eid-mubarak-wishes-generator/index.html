Tips for the Best Eid Message

  • Use their name: "Eid Mubarak, Fatima" hits different than "Eid Mubarak everyone"
  • Match the relationship: Fun and casual for friends, respectful for parents, sweet for kids
  • Add a personal touch: Reference a memory, an inside joke, or something you appreciate
  • Include a dua: A sincere prayer for someone shows you care beyond just words
  • Send it on time: Eid morning messages hit the hardest — don't wait until evening!
