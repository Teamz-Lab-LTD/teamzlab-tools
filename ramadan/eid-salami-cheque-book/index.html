What Is an Eid Salami Cheque Book?

Eid Salami (also called Eidi or Eidiya) is the beloved tradition of giving monetary gifts during Eid celebrations. The Eid Salami Cheque Book Generator turns this tradition into a fun, interactive experience by letting you create beautiful, printable cheques that look like real bank cheques but are themed entirely around Eid festivities.

Instead of handing over cash in a plain envelope, you can present children and family members with an official-looking "Eid Salami Cheque" from the "Bank of Eid Mubarak." Each cheque includes the recipient's name, the gift amount in both numbers and words, a unique cheque number, and a playful watermark that reads "NOT NEGOTIABLE — LOVE & BLESSINGS ONLY." It is the perfect way to make Eid gift-giving memorable and photo-worthy for social media.

How to Use the Eid Salami Cheque Book Generator

Using this tool is simple. Enter the recipient's name, the gift amount, select your currency, add the giver's name, and choose a date. The tool automatically converts the amount into English words and generates a unique cheque number. Pick from three cheque styles: Classic Bank Style for a realistic look, Fun Kids Style with colorful decorations perfect for children, or Elegant Style for a premium presentation.

Click "Add to Cheque Book" to build a collection of cheques for your entire family. When you are ready, use "Print Cheque Book" to print all cheques on one page, or download individual cheques as PNG images to share on WhatsApp, Instagram, or TikTok. Everything runs in your browser with zero data collection — your family names and amounts stay completely private.

Who Is This Tool For?

This tool is designed for Muslim families celebrating Eid ul Fitr or Eid ul Adha anywhere in the world. Parents and grandparents giving Salami to children, uncles and aunts distributing Eidi at family gatherings, and friends exchanging symbolic gifts will all find this tool adds a delightful touch to the occasion. Teachers can use it in Islamic school Eid parties, and community organizers can create cheques for charity drives. The generator supports nine currencies covering South Asia, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Europe, and North America.
