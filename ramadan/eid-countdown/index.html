When Is Eid ul-Fitr 2026?

Eid ul-Fitr 2026 is expected on March 30 or 31, depending on the sighting of the Shawwal moon. In Bangladesh, the date is typically announced by the National Moon Sighting Committee on the 29th of Ramadan. Saudi Arabia and other Middle Eastern countries may celebrate a day earlier. The date shown in this countdown is an estimate based on astronomical calculations — the actual date will be confirmed closer to Eid. Many Muslims around the world eagerly await the moon sighting announcement that marks the end of Ramadan and the beginning of Eid celebrations.

What to Do Before Eid

Prepare for Eid by paying Fitrah (Sadaqatul Fitr) before the Eid prayer, planning your Eid outfit, buying gifts for family and friends, preparing special food and sweets, decorating your home, and making a list of people to visit. The last 10 days of Ramadan are especially blessed — spend them in extra worship, charity, and seeking Laylatul Qadr.
