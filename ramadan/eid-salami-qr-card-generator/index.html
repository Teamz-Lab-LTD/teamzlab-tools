What Is the Eid Salami QR Card?

The Eid Salami QR Card Generator creates personalized digital cards that combine the traditional spirit of Eid Salami with modern mobile banking. In Bangladesh, the tradition of giving Eid Salami (monetary gifts) has evolved with the rise of mobile wallets like bKash, Nagad, and Rocket. Instead of exchanging crisp banknotes in person, millions now send and receive Salami digitally.

This tool lets you create a professional-looking Salami card featuring your photo, a scannable QR code containing your mobile wallet number, and a festive Eid greeting in Bangla. Share the card on Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, or any social media platform, and your friends and family can scan the QR code to send you Eid Salami instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the QR code work?

The QR code encodes your mobile wallet number. When someone scans it with their phone camera or any QR scanner app, they will see your number and can use it to send Salami through their mobile wallet app.

What mobile wallets are supported?

The tool supports bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, Tap, and any other mobile wallet. Simply select your provider and enter your number. The QR code works universally regardless of which wallet you use.

Is my photo and number safe?

Absolutely. Everything runs in your browser. Your photo is processed locally and the QR code is generated using JavaScript on your device. No data is ever sent to any server.

Can I download and print the card?

Yes! The card is generated at 1080x1080 pixels, perfect for both social media and printing. Click the Download button to save it as a PNG image.

What is the best size for my photo?

Any clear photo works. The tool automatically crops and positions your photo on the card. A well-lit portrait photo with a clean background works best.

Can I use this for Eid ul Adha too?

Yes! The card design works for both Eid ul Fitr and Eid ul Adha. The Eid greeting is universal.

When is Eid ul Fitr 2026?

Eid ul Fitr 2026 is expected around March 19-20, 2026, depending on moon sighting. Create and share your Salami card before Eid to maximize digital Salami gifts!
