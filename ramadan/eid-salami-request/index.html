What Is the Eid Salami Request Generator?

The Eid Salami Request Generator is a fun, viral tool that lets you create hilarious request cards asking your family and friends to send you Eid Salami. In Bangladeshi culture, Salami is the beloved tradition of giving monetary gifts during Eid, and this tool turns the act of requesting Salami into a shareable, laugh-out-loud social media moment.

Choose from over 20 pre-made funny templates in both Bangla and English, or write your own custom message. The tool generates a beautiful card with festive Eid decorations, your funny request, and optionally your mobile wallet number. Download the card as an image, share on WhatsApp, or copy a share link that takes people directly to your request on our website.

How to Use the Salami Request Generator

Enter your name, optionally add your bKash or Nagad number, and either pick a funny template or write your own message. The canvas generates a beautiful Eid-themed request card in real time. Click Download to save as a high-quality PNG, share on WhatsApp with one tap, or use the Share Link button for a viral URL that shows your request when opened. The funnier your message, the more Salami you will collect!

Tips for Maximum Salami Collection

  • Use funny Bangla templates for family groups and English for friends abroad
  • Share on WhatsApp status, Facebook story, and Instagram story simultaneously
  • Add your bKash/Nagad number so people can actually send you Salami
  • Tag specific relatives in your posts for maximum guilt-trip effect
  • Send personalized requests to rich uncles and aunties individually

Frequently Asked Questions

Can people send me real money through this?

This tool creates fun greeting cards only. To receive actual Salami, add your mobile wallet number to the card or use our QR Card Generator.

Can I write my own message?

Yes! While we provide 20+ funny templates, you can type any custom message in Bangla, English, or any language.

Is my wallet number safe?

Everything runs in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your number only appears on the card you generate.

Can I use this for Eid ul Adha?

Absolutely! The Salami request works for both Eid ul Fitr and Eid ul Adha.

How do I share on social media?

Download the card image and post it to any platform, or use the Share Link for a clickable URL that shows your card on our website.

What makes a good Salami request?

Humor! The funnier and more dramatic your request, the more likely people are to engage. Guilt-tripping uncles and using dramatic Bangla is a proven strategy.
