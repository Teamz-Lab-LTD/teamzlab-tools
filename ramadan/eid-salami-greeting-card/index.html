What Is the Eid Salami Greeting Card Maker?

This tool creates beautiful, shareable Eid Salami greeting cards inspired by the digital Salami culture in Bangladesh. With millions of people sending and receiving Eid Salami through mobile wallets, sharing a festive greeting card with your Salami has become a beloved tradition on social media. This generator lets you create professional-quality cards with custom amounts, personal messages in Bangla, and your signature — all rendered on a beautiful canvas with Eid-themed illustrations.

How to Create Your Eid Salami Greeting Card

Enter the Salami amount you are sending or have received. Toggle the "Hide amount" switch if you want to mask it with stars for privacy when sharing publicly. Write a personal message in Bangla or English — funny messages, heartfelt wishes, or inside jokes all work great. Add your name or nickname as the signature. Choose a card theme that matches your style: Warm Golden for a classic Eid feel, Festive Pink for a vibrant look, Eid Green for traditional Islamic aesthetics, or Night Sky for an elegant touch.

The card features hand-drawn style Eid illustrations including two people embracing in the traditional Eid greeting, decorative stars, a gift box, and festive Bangla calligraphy reading "ঈদের চাঁদ আকাশে সালামি দিন" (The moon of Eid is in the sky, give Salami). Everything is rendered at 1200x675 pixels for crisp sharing on Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, and other platforms.

Tips for the Perfect Eid Salami Card

  • Write your message in Bangla for the most authentic and shareable result
  • Use funny or heartfelt messages that friends and family will appreciate
  • Hide the amount when sharing on public social media profiles
  • Download and share before Eid morning for maximum impact
  • Pair with an actual digital Salami transfer for the complete experience

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the card maker work?

Enter your amount, message, and name. The tool generates a beautiful Eid greeting card on a canvas element in your browser. Download it as a PNG image to share anywhere.

Can I write in Bangla?

Yes! The card uses Noto Sans Bengali font and fully supports Bangla text for amount, message, and signature fields.

Can I hide the Salami amount?

Yes, toggle the "Hide amount" switch to replace the number with stars (৳ *****) for privacy when sharing on social media.

1200x675 pixels (16:9 ratio), perfect for social media posts, WhatsApp status, Facebook stories, and Instagram.

Everything runs in your browser. No data is uploaded anywhere. Your message and amount stay completely private on your device.
