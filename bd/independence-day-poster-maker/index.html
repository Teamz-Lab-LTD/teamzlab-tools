Tips for a Great Independence Day Poster

Keep your message short and impactful — "জয় বাংলা" or "স্বাধীনতা দিবসের শুভেচ্ছা" works perfectly. Use a clear, high-quality photo if adding one. For school events, add the school name and class. For organizations, add your logo name and designation. The templates are designed with official Bangladesh flag colors (green #006A4E and red #F42A41) for authentic patriotic feel.
