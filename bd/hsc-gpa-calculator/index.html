Bangladesh HSC Grading System 2026

The Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examination is the gateway to university education in Bangladesh. Conducted by the same education boards as SSC, the HSC uses an identical grading scale but covers more advanced subjects based on the student's chosen group — Science, Commerce, or Arts/Humanities. Your HSC GPA plays a critical role in university admissions.

HSC Grade Scale

The grading scale is the same as SSC: A+ (80-100) = 5.00, A (70-79) = 4.00, A- (60-69) = 3.50, B (50-59) = 3.00, C (40-49) = 2.00, D (33-39) = 1.00, and F (0-32) = 0.00. The overall GPA is calculated as the average of all subject grade points. The 4th subject (optional) is included in the GPA calculation.

University Admission Requirements

Dhaka University requires a combined SSC and HSC GPA of at least 8.00 (out of 10) for most units. BUET requires a minimum GPA of 5.00 in both SSC and HSC with individual subject minimums. Chittagong University, Rajshahi University, and Jahangirnagar University each have their own GPA requirements, generally ranging from 7.00-8.00 combined. Private universities typically have lower GPA requirements, starting from 6.00 combined.

HSC GPA Formula

GPA = (GP of Subject 1 + GP of Subject 2 + ... + GP of Subject N) / N

Combined GPA for University = SSC GPA + HSC GPA (out of 10.00)

Science Group Subjects

HSC Science group includes compulsory subjects (Bangla, English, ICT) and group subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Higher Mathematics). Students must also take an optional 4th subject. All subjects contribute equally to the GPA calculation. Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics marks are particularly important for engineering university admissions.

Commerce and Arts Groups

Commerce group includes Accounting, Business Organization, Finance and Banking, and Economics along with the compulsory subjects. Arts group includes subjects like History, Geography, Civics, Economics, and Sociology. Each group has specific university admission pathways. Commerce students often target BBA programs, while Arts students pursue BA, BSS, and LLB programs.

How to Maximize Your HSC GPA

Focus on grade boundaries. Moving from 79 to 80 in any subject increases your grade point from 4.00 to 5.00. Prioritize subjects where you are close to the next boundary. Practice previous years' questions as HSC exams often repeat question patterns. Ensure you attempt all questions in the written portion, as partial marks can push you across grade boundaries.
