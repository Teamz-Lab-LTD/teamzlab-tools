How to Play Truth or Dare

Take turns choosing "Truth" or "Dare." If you pick Truth, answer the question honestly. If you pick Dare, complete the challenge. Use the intensity filter to match your mood — Sweet for light fun, Flirty for romance, Deep for meaningful conversations, or Spicy for adventurous couples.

Game Rules

Truth: Answer honestly — no dodging!

Dare: Complete the challenge within 2 minutes

Skip: Each player gets 2 free skips per game

No pressure: Both partners should feel comfortable

Why Couples Love Truth or Dare

Truth or Dare helps couples communicate, laugh together, and learn new things about each other — even after years of being together. The game creates a safe space to share feelings, be playful, and build intimacy through vulnerability and fun.

Truth or Dare for Date Night

Set the mood with candles and your favorite drinks. Start with Sweet intensity to warm up, then gradually increase to Flirty or Deep as you get comfortable. The game naturally creates conversation and connection — perfect for a cozy evening at home.

Tips for Playing Together

Respect boundaries — if a question or dare makes either partner uncomfortable, skip it without judgment. The goal is fun and connection, not pressure. Take turns, listen to each other's answers, and use the game as a springboard for deeper conversations.
