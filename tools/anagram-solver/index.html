What Is an Anagram?

An anagram is a word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another word or phrase, using each letter exactly once. For example, "listen" and "silent" are anagrams of each other. Anagrams have been used in literature, puzzles, and word games for centuries. They test vocabulary and pattern recognition skills.
