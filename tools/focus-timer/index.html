Why Use a Focus Timer?

Research consistently shows that focused, uninterrupted work sessions produce dramatically better results than constant multitasking. A focus timer creates structure — you commit to a specific block of time, eliminating the temptation to check email or social media. The timer serves as a commitment device.

Choosing the Right Duration

  • 25 minutes: Classic Pomodoro-style. Great for getting started or when motivation is low.
  • 45 minutes: Ideal for writing, studying, or moderately complex tasks.
  • 60 minutes: For deep work sessions — programming, research, creative projects.
  • Custom: Set any duration that works for your specific task and energy level.

Focus Science

Ultradian rhythms: ~90 min peak focus, then 20 min rest

Average: 2-4 hours of peak cognitive work per day

Tip: Start with shorter sessions, increase as focus stamina builds
