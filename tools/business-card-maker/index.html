Digital Business Cards vs Physical Cards

Business Card Size and Print Specifications

Standard business card dimensions vary by region. In the United States and Canada, the standard size is 3.5 by 2 inches (89 by 51 millimeters). In Europe and most international markets, the ISO standard is 85 by 55 millimeters. Japan uses 91 by 55 millimeters. This tool generates cards at the US standard ratio and downloads at 3x resolution (approximately 1350 by 770 pixels), which exceeds 300 DPI at standard print size. For best results, use a professional print service with at least 300 gsm cardstock. Matte or soft-touch finishes complement the minimalist templates, while glossy or spot UV coatings pair well with the darker templates.
