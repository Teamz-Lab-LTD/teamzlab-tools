Why Track Your Budget?

A budget gives you control over your money instead of wondering where it went. Studies show people who track their spending save 10-15% more than those who don't. This tool makes it simple — add entries, see your balance, and understand your spending patterns by category.

Privacy-First Financial Tracking

Most budget apps require you to create an account and store your financial data on their servers. This tool takes a different approach: everything is stored in your browser's localStorage. Your income, expenses, and spending habits remain 100% private.

Budgeting Basics

Balance = Total Income - Total Expenses

50/30/20 Rule: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings

Savings Rate = (Income - Expenses) / Income x 100%
