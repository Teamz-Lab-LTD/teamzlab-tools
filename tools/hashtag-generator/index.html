Best Hashtag Strategies by Platform

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post, but research shows 8 to 15 well-chosen hashtags perform best. TikTok favors 3 to 5 highly relevant hashtags that match trending sounds and challenges. YouTube hashtags appear above video titles and help with search ranking, with 3 to 5 being the sweet spot. LinkedIn posts perform best with 3 to 5 professional hashtags that match industry topics. Facebook has de-emphasized hashtags, but 1 to 3 relevant tags still help with discoverability in groups and search. X/Twitter works best with 1 to 2 hashtags woven naturally into the tweet text rather than stacked at the end.

Festival and Seasonal Hashtag Tips

Seasonal campaigns like Eid sales, Christmas promotions, Diwali deals, and Black Friday events need hashtags that combine the event name with your product category. Mix trending event hashtags like EidSale2026 or BlackFriday with niche product hashtags for maximum reach. Start using seasonal hashtags 1 to 2 weeks before the event peaks to build momentum. Branded campaign hashtags should be unique, short, and easy to remember. Track which hashtags drive the most engagement and double down on your top performers for future campaigns.
