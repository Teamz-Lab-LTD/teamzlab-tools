How the Love Calculator Works

The love calculator takes two names and computes a compatibility percentage using a character-based algorithm. Each letter in both names is converted to its character code, and the values are combined through a series of mathematical operations including summing, multiplying alternating digits, and applying modular arithmetic. The result is a love score between 0 and 100 percent that stays consistent every time you enter the same two names. It is a fun, lighthearted tool designed for entertainment — not a substitute for real relationship advice.

The algorithm ensures that name order does not matter. Entering "Alice + Bob" produces the same score as "Bob + Alice." This makes it fair and fun to compare results with your partner. The score is generated entirely in your browser with no data sent anywhere, so your romantic curiosity stays completely private.

Love Score Categories Explained

Your love score falls into one of five categories, each with its own meaning and personalized advice. Scores between 0 and 20 percent land in the "Keep Looking" zone, suggesting you might be better as acquaintances. Scores of 21 to 40 percent indicate a "Just Friends" dynamic with potential for growth. The 41 to 60 percent range means "There's a Spark" — real chemistry that could develop into something meaningful. Scores of 61 to 80 percent represent a "Strong Connection" with deep compatibility. And if you hit 81 to 100 percent, congratulations — the calculator declares you soulmates with off-the-charts chemistry.

Each category comes with tailored relationship advice ranging from playful encouragement to over-the-top celebration. The advice is meant to bring a smile and spark conversation, making it perfect for sharing with friends on social media or in group chats.
