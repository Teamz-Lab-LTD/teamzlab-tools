Rotate PDF Pages — Free Online Tool

Rotate PDF pages 90°, 180°, or 270° directly in your browser. Rotate all pages at once or select specific page ranges. Fix upside-down scans, correct landscape pages, and adjust orientation. Free, private, no upload required.

How to Rotate PDF Pages Online

Upload your PDF, select the rotation angle (90° clockwise, 180° flip, or 90° counter-clockwise), choose whether to rotate all pages or specific ones, and click "Rotate & Download." The rotated PDF downloads instantly. Everything runs in your browser using pdf-lib.js — your file is never uploaded to any server. Works on any device with a modern browser.

When to Rotate PDF Pages

Scanned documents often have pages in the wrong orientation. Landscape tables in portrait documents need rotation for better readability. Upside-down fax scans, sideways photos, and incorrectly oriented slides all benefit from page rotation. This tool fixes any of these issues in seconds without re-scanning or re-creating the document.

Rotate Specific Pages in a PDF

Not all pages need rotation? Select "Custom" and enter the specific pages or ranges you want to rotate. Use comma-separated values (1, 3, 5) or ranges (1-5) or combine both (1-3, 7, 10-12). Only the specified pages will be rotated — the rest remain unchanged. This is useful for mixed-orientation documents where only some pages are scanned sideways.

Privacy-First PDF Page Rotation

Your documents are processed entirely in your browser. The pdf-lib.js library reads your PDF into browser memory, applies the rotation to the specified pages, and generates the output file — all without any network requests. When you close the tab, all data is cleared. Safe for contracts, medical records, financial statements, and any sensitive documents.
