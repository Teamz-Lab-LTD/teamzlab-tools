JPG to PDF vs PNG to PDF

Both formats work perfectly for PDF conversion. JPG images are already compressed, so they produce smaller PDFs — ideal for photos and scanned documents. PNG images preserve transparency and sharp text, making them better for screenshots, diagrams, and documents with text. This tool handles both formats and lets you choose the compression quality for the output PDF.
