How the Fake WhatsApp Chat Generator Works

This tool renders a pixel-perfect WhatsApp chat screenshot entirely inside your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. You enter a contact name, choose an online status, add messages, and pick sender roles (you or them). Each message supports custom timestamps and read receipt indicators — single check for sent, double check for delivered, and blue double check for read.

When you click "Generate Preview," the tool draws the WhatsApp interface onto a canvas element: the header bar with back arrow, contact name, and call icons; the chat area with correctly styled message bubbles, tails, and timestamps; and the bottom input bar with camera, attachment, and microphone icons. The result looks virtually identical to a real WhatsApp conversation.

You can switch between WhatsApp's light and dark themes to match whatever screenshot style you need. The generated image can be downloaded as a PNG, copied to your clipboard for instant pasting, or shared via your device's native share dialog.

Privacy and Security

Everything happens on your device. No messages, names, or photos are sent to any server. The canvas rendering is 100% client-side JavaScript. If you upload a profile photo, it stays in your browser's memory only and is discarded when you close the tab. No data is stored in cookies or databases.
