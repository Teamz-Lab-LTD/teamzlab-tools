How AI Translation Works in Your Browser

This translator uses the M2M100 multilingual model through Transformers.js. M2M100 (Many-to-Many) was developed by Meta AI and can translate directly between 100 language pairs without pivoting through English. The model runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly.

When you click "Translate," the model is downloaded once and cached. All subsequent translations happen offline on your device. Your text is never transmitted to any server, making this ideal for translating sensitive documents, personal messages, or confidential business communications.

How It Works

  • Model download: The translation model downloads once (~200-400MB) and is cached in your browser for offline use.
  • Source language: Select the language of your input text, or it will be auto-detected.
  • Neural translation: The M2M100 transformer model translates directly between language pairs without English as an intermediary.
  • Output: The translated text appears in the output box. Copy it or continue editing the source text.

Privacy Advantage Over Cloud Translators

Google Translate, DeepL, and other online translators send your text to remote servers for processing. Even if they claim not to store data, your text traverses the network and could be intercepted or logged. This tool eliminates that risk entirely — the AI model runs on your device, and your text never leaves your browser.
