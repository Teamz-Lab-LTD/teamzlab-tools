    How to Create Fake Instagram DM Screenshots

    This fake Instagram DM generator lets you create realistic-looking direct message conversations without using a real Instagram account. Simply enter the other person's username, upload a profile photo, and start adding messages. You can switch between light and dark mode to match your preferred Instagram theme.

    Each message can be assigned to either "Me" or "Them" with a custom timestamp. Double-tap any message in the preview to toggle a heart reaction — just like the real Instagram app. When you are done, download the conversation as a PNG image or copy it directly to your clipboard for pasting into other apps.

    The tool generates a pixel-perfect Instagram DM preview with the correct header layout, message bubble colors, and bottom input bar. Your messages appear in blue bubbles (sent) and gray bubbles (received), matching Instagram's actual design. The "Seen" and "Delivered" status indicators can be toggled on or off.

    Privacy and Security

    This fake Instagram DM generator runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your messages, photos, and usernames are never uploaded, stored, or logged. The profile photo you upload stays in your browser's memory only for the current session. When you close the tab, everything is gone. This makes it one of the most private fake chat generators available online.

    The download and copy features use your browser's built-in Canvas API to render the preview as a PNG image. No third-party services are involved. You can even use this tool offline once the page has loaded.
