Why Track Your Subscriptions

The average person spends $219/month on subscriptions and most underestimate their spending by 2-3x. Between streaming services, software, gym memberships, and cloud storage, costs add up quietly. This tracker gives you a clear picture of your recurring expenses with total monthly and yearly costs, renewal date warnings, and sorting by cost, date, or category. Seeing all subscriptions in one place makes it easy to identify services you no longer use and cancel them before the next billing cycle.

Privacy-First Subscription Management

Most subscription tracking apps require access to your bank accounts or email to auto-detect charges. This tool takes a different approach. You manually add subscriptions, and everything is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. There is no server, no account, and no data sharing. Your subscription details remain entirely on your device. Export your data to CSV anytime for backup or analysis in a spreadsheet application.

How Subscription Costs Are Calculated

The dashboard automatically converts between monthly and yearly equivalents so you can compare all subscriptions on equal terms. Monthly subscriptions are multiplied by 12 for the yearly total, and yearly subscriptions are divided by 12 for the monthly equivalent. Renewal warnings highlight subscriptions due within 7 days in yellow and overdue ones in red, giving you time to decide whether to cancel before the next charge.
