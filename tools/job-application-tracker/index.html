Why Track Job Applications?

Job searching can be overwhelming. Without a system, it's easy to forget which companies you applied to, miss follow-ups, or lose track of interview dates. This tracker gives you a clear overview of your entire job search in one place.

Keep Your Search Confidential

Your job search is personal. Most tracking tools require accounts and store your career data on their servers. This tool respects your privacy — everything is stored locally in your browser. No one else can see what companies you're applying to.

Job Search Metrics

Response Rate = (Screening + Interview + Offer) / Total Applications x 100%

Interview Rate = (Interview + Offer) / Total Applications x 100%

Average: 10-20% response rate, 2-5% offer rate for online applications
