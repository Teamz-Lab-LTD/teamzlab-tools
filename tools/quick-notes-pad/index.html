How Quick Notes Works

Distraction-notepad that auto-saves to your device. Multiple notes, word count, and download as .txt. No account needed. Use the tool above to get your results instantly — everything runs in your browser with no data sent to any server.

A Truly Private Online Notepad

Most online notepads store your text on their servers, requiring accounts and collecting your data. Quick Notes takes a different approach. Everything is saved in your browser's localStorage, meaning your notes never leave your device. There is no account to create, no cloud sync, and no data collection. Just open the page and start writing. Your notes persist across browser sessions and survive page refreshes, so your thoughts are always where you left them.

Features Built for Writers

Quick Notes includes everything you need for fast, focused note-taking. Auto-save captures every keystroke automatically with debouncing for performance. The tabbed interface lets you create and switch between multiple notes instantly. Real-time word and character counts update as you type. Download any note as a .txt file with one click. The entire tool works offline after the first page load, so you can write anywhere without an internet connection.

When to Use Quick Notes

Quick Notes is ideal for capturing ideas during meetings, drafting quick messages before sending, jotting down phone numbers, keeping a daily journal, or writing todo lists. Because it runs entirely in your browser with no server dependency, it loads instantly and works even on slow connections. Use it as a scratchpad while browsing the web, a place to draft emails, or a lightweight alternative to heavy note-taking applications.
