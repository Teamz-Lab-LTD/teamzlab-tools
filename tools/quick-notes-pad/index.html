A Truly Private Notepad

Most online notepads store your text on their servers. Quick Notes is different — everything is saved in your browser's localStorage. Your notes never leave your device. No account, no sync, no data collection. Just a clean writing space that remembers your notes.

Features

  • Auto-save: Every keystroke is saved automatically (debounced for performance)
  • Multiple notes: Create and switch between notes using tabs
  • Word and character count: Real-time count displayed below the editor
  • Download: Export any note as a .txt file
  • Works offline: After first load, works completely without internet
  • Distraction-free: Minimal design focused on writing
