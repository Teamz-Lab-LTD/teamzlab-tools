How the World Clock Works

This world clock displays the current time in multiple cities simultaneously, updating every second. Add any timezone from the dropdown to compare times across the globe. Your local time is shown first and highlighted. All time calculations use your browser's built-in timezone support for accuracy.

Popular Time Zones

EST/EDT: New York, Toronto (UTC-5/-4)

PST/PDT: Los Angeles, Vancouver (UTC-8/-7)

GMT/BST: London (UTC+0/+1)

CET/CEST: Berlin, Paris (UTC+1/+2)

JST: Tokyo (UTC+9)

IST: Mumbai, Delhi (UTC+5:30)

World Clock for Remote Teams

Remote teams spread across time zones need to coordinate meetings, standups, and deadlines. This world clock lets you see everyone's local time at a glance. Add your team members' cities and find overlapping work hours for scheduling. No more accidentally calling someone at 3 AM.

World Clock for Travel

Traveling internationally? Use this tool to track time at home and your destination simultaneously. Know when to call home, when business hours are, and when your flight's local arrival time corresponds to your body clock. It helps with jet lag planning and staying connected across time zones.
