How This Free SOP Maker Works

This SOP maker runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, and the File API. When you upload a screenshot, it is read locally using FileReader — the file never leaves your device. Annotations are drawn on an HTML5 Canvas overlay, and the final guide is rendered client-side using html2canvas for PNG export or jsPDF for PDF generation. No server, no account, no data collection.

Tips for Writing Effective SOPs

Start each step with an action verb: "Click", "Navigate", "Enter", "Select". Keep descriptions concise — one action per step. Use screenshots to show exactly where to click or what the screen should look like. Add annotations (arrows, rectangles) to highlight the important areas. Number your steps sequentially and give your SOP a clear, searchable title.

Good SOPs reduce support tickets, speed up onboarding, and create institutional knowledge that survives employee turnover. The investment in documenting a process once pays dividends every time someone new needs to follow it.
